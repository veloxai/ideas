## üéØ Overview

Uniting Doctors is a social media platform designed exclusively for the medical community‚Äîfrom nurses to surgeons, teachers to researchers. The platform enables medical professionals worldwide to share, discuss, and verify the latest medical news, research findings, and clinical insights, bypassing bureaucratic delays to keep critical information at the forefront of the industry.

### Core Mission

**Breaking down barriers to medical knowledge dissemination** by creating a trusted, verified community where medical professionals can:

- Share breaking medical news and research findings in real-time
- Discuss off-label medication uses and clinical recommendations
- Request and provide peer recommendations on complex cases
- Aggregate data from publications, government bodies, and trusted sources
- Rate, comment, and verify medical information with supporting evidence
- Maintain professional anonymity when needed while building reputation

### Origin Story: AATD Awareness

Inspired by the lack of awareness around Alpha-1 Antitrypsin Deficiency (AATD), this platform aims to ensure that rare diseases and critical medical information never remain hidden due to information silos or bureaucratic processes.

## üåü Key Features

### Core Social Features

- **News Feed**: Personalized feed based on specialties, interests, and connections
- **Post Types**: Text, images, research papers, case studies, polls, questions
- **Engagement**: Like, comment, share, save, and follow
- **Direct Messaging**: Secure peer-to-peer communication
- **Groups**: Specialty-based communities (cardiology, oncology, rare diseases, etc.)
- **Events**: Conferences, webinars, and continuing education opportunities

### Research & Knowledge Management

- **Research Aggregation**: Automatic feeds from PubMed, medical journals, and research databases
- **Literature Library**: Personal research library with annotations and sharing
- **Clinical Guidelines**: Curated guidelines from medical associations and government bodies
- **Case Repository**: Anonymized case studies with outcomes and learnings

### Verification & Trust Systems

- **Evidence-Based Discussions**: Attach sources, citations, and supporting data to posts
- **Peer Review**: Community rating system for content accuracy and relevance
- **Expert Verification**: Verified badges for specialists in each field
- **Fact-Checking**: AI-assisted fact-checking against medical databases
- **Reputation Score**: Cumulative score based on contributions and peer ratings

### Professional Tools

- **Off-Label Database**: Crowdsourced database of off-label medication uses with outcomes
- **Peer Consultation**: Request recommendations from specialists anonymously
- **Research Collaboration**: Connect researchers with shared interests
- **CME/CE Credits**: Earn continuing education credits for participation
- **Career Resources**: Job board, residency programs, and professional development

### Privacy & Anonymity

- **Anonymous Posting**: Option to post anonymously while maintaining reputation
- **Reputation Attribution**: Reputation points attributed to user even when posting anonymously
- **Secure Identity**: Strong verification without compromising anonymity
- **Data Protection**: End-to-end encryption for sensitive communications

## üí° Unique Value Propositions

### 1. **Speed to Information**

Traditional medical information dissemination can take months or years. Uniting Doctors enables real-time sharing while maintaining professional standards.

### 2. **Democratic Knowledge Access**

No more information silos. All medical professionals, regardless of location or institution, have equal access to the latest information.

### 3. **Global Collaboration**

Break down geographical barriers‚Äîa nurse in Iowa can learn from a surgeon in Singapore.

### 4. **Rare Disease Advocacy**

Special focus on rare and under-diagnosed conditions like AATD, ensuring these diseases get the attention they deserve.

### 5. **Evidence-Based Community**

Unlike general social media, every claim can be challenged, supported, and verified by the community with citations and data.

### 6. **Professional Anonymity**

Medical professionals can discuss controversial topics, errors, and learning experiences without career risk.

- **Hosting**: AWS or Cloudflare

- **Monitoring**: DataDog or New Relic
- **Analytics**: Mixpanel + custom dashboard

### Integrations

- **Medical Databases**: PubMed API, Crossref API
- **News Aggregation**: RSS feeds, custom scrapers
- **Verification**: Professional license verification services
- **Email**: SendGrid or AWS SES
