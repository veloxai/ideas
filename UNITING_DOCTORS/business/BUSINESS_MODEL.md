## üö® Risk Mitigation

### Low Initial Adoption

- **Risk**: Not enough users to create network effects
- **Mitigation**: Invite-only beta, targeted outreach, partner with institutions

### Competition from Established Players

- **Risk**: LinkedIn, Doximity, or Sermo improve their offerings
- **Mitigation**: Focus on unique features (anonymity, research integration, off-label database)

### Content Moderation Challenges

- **Risk**: Misinformation or inappropriate content
- **Mitigation**: AI + human moderation, community reporting, strict guidelines

### Revenue Model Rejection

- **Risk**: Users unwilling to pay for premium features
- **Mitigation**: Strong free tier, clear value proposition, institutional focus

### Regulatory Challenges

- **Risk**: HIPAA, international regulations
- **Mitigation**: Legal counsel, compliance-first approach, insurance
