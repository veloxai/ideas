## ğŸ”’ Patient Privacy (HIPAA Compliance)

### Absolute Rules

**NEVER share**:
- Patient names or identifiers
- Specific dates (use "recently" or "several months ago")
- Exact ages (use age ranges: "50s", "pediatric")
- Geographic specifics (use regions: "Midwest", "rural clinic")
- Rare identifying details (unusual combinations)

**When Sharing Cases**:
- Anonymize completely
- Change non-essential details
- Use generic descriptions
- Focus on clinical learning points
- When in doubt, don't post

**Violations Result In**:
- Immediate content removal
- Account suspension or ban
- Possible legal action
- Report to licensing boards

## ğŸ” Off-Label Medication Discussions

### Guidelines for Off-Label Discussions

**Allowed**:
- Sharing clinical experiences with off-label uses
- Discussing published research on off-label uses
- Asking for peer experiences with specific off-label uses
- Citing outcomes (positive, neutral, negative)

**Required**:
- Clearly state "off-label use"
- Provide context (condition, dosage, duration)
- Include citations when available
- Disclose conflicts of interest

**Prohibited**:
- Recommending off-label use without evidence
- Promoting unproven treatments
- Financial incentives for promoting medications
- Discouraging proven treatments
