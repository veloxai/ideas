## Executive Summary

This section presents three budget scenarios based on grant size, each designed to achieve maximum social impact within available resources. All scenarios prioritize:

1. **Platform development** (core infrastructure, security, features)
2. **AATD awareness campaign** (educational outreach, partnerships)
3. **Research and evaluation** (measuring impact, publishing findings)
4. **Sustainability planning** (path to long-term financial viability)

**Funding Request Range**: $500,000 - $2,000,000 for 12-24 months

- Cloud costs may be lower initially (fewer users than projected)
- Personnel costs may shift (contractor vs. FTE trade-offs)
- Program costs may reallocate (more conferences, less advertising)

**Process for Budget Modifications**:

- <10% reallocation within categories: Leadership approval
- 10-20% reallocation: Funder notification
- > 20% reallocation or new budget line: Funder pre-approval required

**Contingency Usage Priorities**:

1. Security incidents or compliance issues (highest priority)
2. User growth exceeding projections (scaling infrastructure)
3. Opportunity investments (high-impact partnerships)
4. Team expansion (if growth accelerates)

## Cost Efficiency Measures

**How We Keep Costs Low**:

1. **Remote-First Organization**
   - No office lease (save $50-100K/year)
   - Hire talent anywhere (not limited to expensive cities)
   - Flexible workforce (contractors for specialized skills)

2. **Open-Source Technology**
   - PostgreSQL, MongoDB, Redis (free vs. proprietary)
   - Next.js, React Native (free frameworks)
   - Linux servers (no Windows licensing)

3. **Cloud Optimization**
   - Right-sizing instances (don't over-provision)
   - Reserved instances (40-60% discount vs. on-demand)
   - Auto-scaling (scale down during low traffic)
   - S3 lifecycle policies (archive old data)

4. **Volunteer Community Moderators**
   - 50-150 volunteer moderators by Year 2
   - Saves $200-500K/year vs. paid moderation team

5. **Strategic Partnerships**
   - Medical societies promote for free (mutual benefit)
   - Medical schools provide student users (pipeline)
   - Patient advocacy orgs provide content (mission-aligned)

6. **Phased Feature Development**
   - MVP first, advanced features later (validate demand)
   - A/B test features before full build (avoid waste)
   - User feedback prioritizes roadmap (build what matters)
