## ğŸ” Safety & Compliance

### Patient Privacy

- All cases must be fully de-identified
- Automatic PHI detection before posting
- Geographic data limited to region/state
- No identifying hospital/clinic names

### Professional Liability

- **Disclaimer**: All consultations are peer discussion, not formal medical advice
- **Documentation**: Clear that consulting physician maintains responsibility
- **Professional Insurance**: Recommended for participants
- **Good Samaritan Principles**: Peer assistance in good faith

### Quality Control

- **Verified Users Only**: Emergency features require verification
- **Reputation Requirements**: Minimum reputation to request urgent consultations
- **Response Quality Ratings**: Poor responses flagged
- **Moderator Oversight**: 24/7 moderation for emergency features
