## Overview: Phased Launch Approach

Uniting Doctors employs a carefully orchestrated go-to-market strategy designed to build **community trust, viral growth, and sustainable adoption** within the medical professional community. Our approach prioritizes **quality over quantity**, starting with engaged early adopters and expanding systematically.

### Strategic Framework

**Phase 0: Pre-Launch** (Months -3 to 0)

- Market validation and user research
- Beta user recruitment (500 users)
- Partnership development
- Content creation and brand building

**Phase 1: Soft Launch** (Months 1-3)

- Invite-only expansion (1,000 â†’ 2,500 users)
- AATD awareness campaign kickoff
- Community building and feedback loops
- Infrastructure stress testing

**Phase 2: Public Launch** (Months 4-6)

- Open registration with verification
- Professional tier launch
- Press and media coverage
- Accelerated user acquisition (2,500 â†’ 5,000 users)

**Phase 3: Growth** (Months 7-12)

- Specialty expansion beyond AATD
- International user acquisition
- Institutional sales program
- Research integration features

**Phase 4: Scale** (Years 2-3)

- Geographic expansion (UK, Canada, Australia)
- Mobile app launch
- CME integration
- Advanced features and partnerships

## Phase 0: Pre-Launch Preparation

**Objective**: Validate product-market fit and refine value proposition

- Feature comparison matrix: Doximity, Sermo, Figure 1, LinkedIn
