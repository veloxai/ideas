- Organizational structure evolution (5 → 50 people)

- Change management and transformation strategies

## Purpose

Organizational development enables Fair Underwriting to:

1. **Scale Without Chaos** - Clear structure as team grows 10x
2. **Maintain Culture** - Preserve startup culture while professionalizing
3. **Ramp New Hires Fast** - 60-day time to productivity (vs 180-day industry average)
4. **Reduce Turnover** - >85% retention (vs 70% industry average)
5. **Develop Leaders** - Internal promotion pipeline for VP+ roles

## Key Frameworks

### Organizational Stages

- **Stage 1: Startup (Months 0-6, 5-10 people)** - Flat, everyone does everything
- **Stage 2: Early Growth (Months 7-12, 10-15 people)** - Functional leads emerge
- **Stage 3: Departmental (Year 2, 15-35 people)** - VPs manage teams
- **Stage 4: Matrix (Year 3, 35-50+ people)** - Regional teams + centers of excellence

### Core Values

- **Patient Privacy First** - HIPAA compliance is non-negotiable
- **Clinical Excellence** - Genetic counseling quality over speed
- **Transparency** - Open communication, default to sharing
- **Move Fast (But Don't Break HIPAA)** - Startup speed with healthcare rigor

### Onboarding Philosophy

- **Day 1-30: Learn** - Understand company, product, role
- **Day 31-60: Contribute** - Ship first meaningful work
- **Day 61-90: Own** - Work autonomously, mentor next hire
