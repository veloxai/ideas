# Research Platform

This folder contains **detailed technical specifications** for Fair Underwriting's research and patient registry features (#21A-21C).

## What Goes Here

**Research infrastructure specifications** including:

- Patient registry architecture and data models
- Research study protocols and workflows
- Consent management systems
- Real-world evidence (RWE) generation capabilities
- Multi-site collaboration tools
- Data standardization and interoperability
- Research analytics and dashboards

## Purpose

The research platform enables Fair Underwriting to:

1. **Accelerate Discovery** - Generate insights from real-world patient data
2. **Support Clinical Trials** - Match patients to trials, collect outcomes
3. **Advance Science** - Contribute to AATD research community
4. **Demonstrate Value** - Show ROI to pharma partners and regulators
5. **Improve Care** - Use data to optimize treatment protocols

## Key Stakeholders

- **Pharmaceutical Partners** - Access to AATD patient cohorts for trials
- **Academic Researchers** - Collaboration on natural history studies
- **Regulatory Agencies** - Real-world evidence for FDA submissions
- **Patient Advocacy Groups** - AlphaNet, Alpha-1 Foundation partnerships
- **Healthcare Systems** - Data-driven care optimization

## Document Format

Each research spec follows this structure:

1. **Research Feature Overview** - What research capability it enables
2. **Scientific Value** - How it advances AATD research
3. **Technical Architecture** - Data models, APIs, workflows
4. **Compliance Requirements** - IRB, consent, privacy protections
5. **Partner Integration** - How pharma/academic partners access data
6. **Success Metrics** - Publications, trial enrollments, insights generated
7. **Investment & Timeline** - Cost and implementation schedule
