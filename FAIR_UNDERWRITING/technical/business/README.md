**Business operations system specifications** including:

- Sales and business development automation
- Marketing and growth tools
- Legal and compliance systems
- Finance and accounting infrastructure
- Human resources and talent management
- Product management and roadmap planning
- Executive operations and board management

## Purpose

Business operations systems enable Fair Underwriting to:

1. **Drive Revenue** - Efficient sales and marketing execution
2. **Manage Compliance** - Legal and regulatory risk management
3. **Control Finances** - Accurate accounting and financial planning
4. **Attract Talent** - Streamlined recruiting and onboarding
5. **Execute Strategy** - Product roadmap and OKR alignment

## Key Systems

- **Sales**: Salesforce CRM, DocuSign, PandaDoc
- **Marketing**: HubSpot, Google Analytics, SEMrush
- **Legal**: Ironclad (contracts), OneTrust (compliance)
- **Finance**: NetSuite ERP, QuickBooks, Carta (cap table)
- **HR**: BambooHR, Greenhouse (recruiting), Lattice (performance)
- **Product**: Productboard, Jira, Figma, Amplitude
- **Executive**: Notion, Asana, Google Workspace

## Document Format

Each business operations spec follows this structure:

1. **Business Function Overview** - What business capability it enables
2. **Team Structure** - Roles, headcount, reporting lines
3. **Systems & Tools** - Software platforms and integrations
4. **Workflows & Processes** - How work gets done
5. **KPIs & Metrics** - How success is measured
6. **Cost Model** - People costs + software costs
7. **Implementation Timeline** - Hiring and system rollout schedule
