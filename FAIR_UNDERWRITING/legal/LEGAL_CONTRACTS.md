---

### Key Terms & Provisions

#### 1.1 Service Description

Provider agrees to furnish genetic monitoring services including:

- Alpha-1 Antitrypsin (AAT) level testing
- SERPINA1 gene mutation analysis
- Customer health monitoring portal
- Aggregate statistical reporting

#### 1.2 Data Privacy & Protection (CRITICAL)

**Genetic Data Restrictions**:

```
Partner explicitly acknowledges and agrees that:

a) NO individual genetic data shall be shared with Partner
b) NO individual test results shall be accessible to Partner
c) NO SERPINA1 gene mutation information shall be used for
   actuarial calculations, underwriting decisions, or policy pricing
d) Partner shall receive ONLY aggregated, anonymized statistics
e) Patent protection (Patent #[NUMBER]) prohibits actuarial use
   of SERPINA1A genetic data
```

**Customer Data Ownership**:

- Customers retain full ownership of their genetic data
- Customers control all access to their individual results
- Provider acts as custodian, not owner, of genetic information

#### 1.3 Customer Enrollment

**Minimum Commitment**:

- Initial: 100 enrolled customers within 90 days
- Year 1: 500 enrolled customers
- Year 2: 1,000 enrolled customers
- Failure to meet minimums: 30-day cure period or termination option

**Enrollment Process**:

- Partner provides customer contact information (name, email, DOB)
- Provider sends enrollment invitation
- Customer provides explicit informed consent
- Customer controls opt-in and opt-out at any time

#### 1.4 Pricing & Payment Terms

**Pricing Structure**:

- **Base Package**: $[AMOUNT]/customer/year
- **Volume Discounts**: As per Exhibit A
- **DNA Sequencing**: $[AMOUNT]/customer (optional add-on)

**Payment Terms**:

- Invoicing: Monthly in arrears
- Payment due: Net 30 days
- Late payment: 1.5% monthly interest
- Payment method: ACH, wire, or check

**Price Protection**:

- Pricing guaranteed for initial [X]-year term
- Annual adjustments not to exceed CPI + 2%

#### 1.5 Service Level Agreements (SLAs)

**Test Turnaround**:

- AAT level test: 3-5 business days (95% of tests)
- SERPINA1 analysis: 10-14 business days
- Critical result notification: Within 4 hours

**System Availability**:

- Partner dashboard: 99.5% uptime
- Customer portal: 99.9% uptime
- API availability: 99.9% uptime

**Support Response**:

- Critical issues: 1 hour response, 4 hour resolution
- Standard issues: 24 hour response, 72 hour resolution
- Non-urgent: 48 hour response

**Remedies for SLA Breach**:

- <99% uptime: 5% monthly fee credit
- <97% uptime: 10% monthly fee credit
- <95% uptime: 20% monthly fee credit + termination right

#### 1.6 Partner Obligations

**Marketing & Promotion**:

- Active promotion to eligible customers
- Educational materials distribution
- Fair Underwriting badge display (if qualified)
- Minimum [X] marketing touchpoints per quarter

**Data Security**:

- Maintain SOC 2 Type II compliance
- Report security incidents within 24 hours
- Annual security audit (shared with Provider)
- Employee training on genetic privacy

**Compliance**:

- Adhere to GINA regulations
- Maintain HIPAA Business Associate Agreement
- Comply with state genetic testing laws
- Avoid genetic discrimination practices

#### 1.7 Intellectual Property

**Patent Rights**:

- Provider retains all rights to patents
- Partner license to offer services (non-exclusive)
- No sublicensing rights
- Termination of license upon agreement end

**Trademarks**:

- Fair Underwriting marks: Licensed for approved uses only
- Partner marks: Licensed to Provider for co-branding
- Badge usage: As per Certification Badge Guidelines

**Data Rights**:

- Customer genetic data: Customer-owned
- Aggregate statistics: Provider-owned
- Research data: Provider-owned (anonymized)

#### 1.8 Indemnification

**Provider Indemnifies Partner Against**:

- Genetic data breaches (Provider fault)
- Lab errors and test result inaccuracies
- Violation of customer privacy rights
- Patent infringement claims

**Partner Indemnifies Provider Against**:

- Misuse of customer data by Partner
- False marketing claims by Partner
- Genetic discrimination by Partner
- Violations of enrollment procedures

**Mutual Indemnification**:

- Third-party IP claims
- Breach of contract claims

#### 1.9 Term & Termination

**Initial Term**: [3-5] years from Effective Date

**Renewal**: Automatic 1-year renewals unless:

- Either party provides 180-day written notice
- Material breach not cured within 60 days
- Mutual agreement to terminate

**Termination for Cause**:

- Material breach after 60-day cure period
- Insolvency or bankruptcy
- Loss of required licenses/certifications
- Genetic discrimination violation (immediate)

**Effect of Termination**:

- Enrolled customers: 180-day wind-down period
- Outstanding payments: Immediately due
- Data return/destruction: Within 30 days
- Badge removal: Immediate

#### 1.10 Limitation of Liability

**Liability Cap**: Total fees paid in prior 12 months

**Exceptions** (Unlimited Liability):

- Genetic data breaches (willful misconduct)
- Genetic discrimination by Partner
- IP infringement
- Gross negligence

#### 1.11 Dispute Resolution

**Process**:

1. Good faith negotiation (30 days)
2. Mediation (60 days)
3. Binding arbitration (JAMS rules)

**Governing Law**: [State] law
**Venue**: [City, State]

#### 1.12 Regulatory Compliance

**HIPAA Business Associate Agreement**: Attached as Exhibit B

**GINA Compliance**: Partner certifies:

- No use of genetic information for underwriting
- No premium adjustments based on genetic data
- No policy cancellations based on genetic data
- Compliance training for all employees

#### 1.13 Confidentiality

**Mutual Non-Disclosure**:

- Term: During agreement + 5 years after
- Exceptions: Legal requirements, public information
- Return of confidential information upon termination

## 4. Customer Terms of Service

### FAIR UNDERWRITING TERMS OF SERVICE

**Last Updated**: [Date]

#### 4.1 Service Description

Fair Underwriting provides genetic testing and health monitoring services including:

- Alpha-1 Antitrypsin level testing
- SERPINA1 gene analysis
- Full DNA sequencing (optional)
- Health insights and recommendations
- Genetic counseling access

#### 4.2 Eligibility

**Requirements**:

- 18+ years of age (or parental consent)
- US resident (initially)
- Valid identification
- Enrollment through partner or direct

#### 4.3 Sample Collection & Testing

**Customer Responsibilities**:

- Provide accurate health information
- Follow sample collection instructions
- Return samples within specified timeframe
- Update contact information

**Provider Responsibilities**:

- Quality sample processing
- Accurate test results
- Timely result delivery
- Result interpretation support

#### 4.4 Payment Terms

**Partner-Sponsored Testing**:

- No cost to customer (partner pays)
- Customer may upgrade to premium services

**Direct-to-Consumer**:

- Payment required before sample processing
- Accepted methods: Credit card, HSA/FSA
- Refund policy: See Section 4.12

#### 4.5 Results Delivery

**Timeline**:

- AAT test: 3-5 business days
- SERPINA1 analysis: 10-14 business days
- Full DNA sequencing: 4-8 weeks

**Delivery Method**:

- Secure customer portal
- Email notification (result ready)
- Optional: Mail (printed copy)
- Mobile app access

#### 4.6 Data Privacy & Ownership (CRITICAL)

**You Own Your Genetic Data**:

- You retain full ownership of your genetic information
- We act as custodian, not owner
- You control who can access your data
- You can download or delete your data at any time

**What We Share**:

- **With You**: All your test results and genetic information
- **With Partners**: ONLY aggregated statistics (no individual data)
- **For Research**: ONLY if you opt-in, and fully anonymized
- **With Healthcare Providers**: ONLY with your explicit consent

**What We Never Share**:

- Individual genetic data with insurance partners
- SERPINA1 mutation status with insurance partners
- Any data that could lead to genetic discrimination

#### 4.7 Consent & Authorizations

**Required Consent**:

- Genetic testing authorization
- Results delivery
- Data storage and security
- Terms of Service acceptance

**Optional Consent**:

- Research participation (anonymized data)
- Marketing communications
- Third-party app integrations
- Family member data sharing

**Withdrawal of Consent**:

- Can be withdrawn at any time
- 30-day processing period
- Data deletion options available

#### 4.8 Genetic Counseling

**Included Services**:

- Initial consultation (30 minutes)
- Result interpretation
- Risk assessment discussion
- Family planning information

**Additional Services** (Fee-based):

- Extended consultations
- Family consultations
- Follow-up sessions
- Specialist referrals

#### 4.9 Limitations of Testing

**What Tests Can Tell You**:

- Your SERPINA1 genotype
- Your AAT levels over time
- Genetic risk factors (if DNA sequencing)
- Carrier status for certain conditions

**What Tests Cannot Tell You**:

- Guaranteed health outcomes
- When/if you'll develop disease
- Complete health risk profile
- Response to all medications

**Not Diagnostic**:

- Results are informational, not diagnostic
- Consult healthcare provider for medical decisions
- Results don't replace medical care

#### 4.10 Prohibited Uses

**You May Not**:

- Share portal login credentials
- Submit someone else's sample without consent
- Use service for non-health purposes
- Attempt to reverse-engineer our technology
- Violate any applicable laws

#### 4.11 Account Termination

**You May Terminate**:

- At any time, for any reason
- Request data deletion
- Export data before deletion

**We May Terminate**:

- Violation of Terms of Service
- Fraudulent activity
- Non-payment (direct customers)
- Illegal use of service

#### 4.12 Refund Policy

**Refunds Available**:

- Sample not yet processed: Full refund
- Test failed (our error): Full refund + retest
- Service issue: Partial refund (case-by-case)

**No Refunds**:

- Test completed successfully
- Customer error (wrong sample, etc.)
- Change of mind after results delivered

#### 4.13 Disclaimer of Warranties

**Service Provided "AS IS"**:

- No guarantee of specific outcomes
- Test accuracy: >99% but not 100%
- System availability: Best effort

#### 4.14 Limitation of Liability

**Our Liability Limited To**:

- Amount you paid for services
- Direct damages only
- No consequential damages

**Exceptions** (Unlimited Liability):

- Data breach (our gross negligence)
- Willful misconduct
- Fraud

#### 4.15 Dispute Resolution

**Process**:

1. Contact customer support (30 days)
2. Mediation (optional)
3. Binding arbitration
4. Class action waiver

**Exception**: Small claims court option

#### 4.16 Changes to Terms

**Notification**:

- 30-day notice for material changes
- Email notification
- Continued use = acceptance

#### 6.3 Information Sharing

**We Share With**:

- **Labs**: For test processing only
- **Genetic Counselors**: For your consultations

- **Partners**: ONLY aggregate statistics (no PII)
- **Researchers**: ONLY if you opt-in, fully anonymized

- Your data for marketing by third parties

- Access all your data
- Download your data

- Regular security audits

**Physical Safeguards**:

- Secure data centers
- Limited personnel access
- Background checks for staff

**Administrative Safeguards**:

- Privacy training for all staff

- Incident response procedures
