| **Chatbot**     | FAQ, simple questions                                | 24/7                           | Friendly, helpful               |

---

### Messaging Principles

1. **Clarity**: Plain language (8th-grade reading level), avoid medical jargon
2. **Empathy**: Acknowledge emotions, provide reassurance
3. **Action-Oriented**: Clear next steps, avoid overwhelming
4. **Privacy**: Reinforce privacy protections (patent framework, data ownership)
5. **Hope**: Emphasize manageability, not doom ("You can reduce your risk")
