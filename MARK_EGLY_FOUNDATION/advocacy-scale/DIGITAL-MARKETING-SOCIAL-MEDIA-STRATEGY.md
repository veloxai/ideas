## Digital Marketing Landscape

### Multi-Channel Approach

**Foundation's Digital Ecosystem**:

- **Owned Media** (Website, email list, social profiles‚ÄîFoundation controls 100%)
- **Earned Media** (Press coverage, shares, word-of-mouth‚Äîcredibility, no cost)
- **Paid Media** (Google Ads, Facebook Ads, influencer sponsorships‚Äîscale, targeting)

**Why Digital Marketing + SEO?**:

- ‚úÖ **SEO = Long-term** (takes 12-18 months‚Äîranks #1, free traffic forever, slow build)
- ‚úÖ **Paid = Immediate** (launch campaign today‚Äîtraffic tomorrow, fast results, but costs continue)
- ‚úÖ **Combined = Optimal** (paid drives traffic while SEO builds‚ÄîYear 1 60% paid / 40% organic ‚Üí Year 5 30% paid / 70% organic, shift over time)

---

### Target Audiences (Different Platforms, Different Audiences)

**Audience 1: Undiagnosed Patients (Priority #1)**

- **Who**: 90,000 Americans with AATD symptoms (early COPD, liver disease‚Äîdon't know it's genetic)
- **Where They Are**: Google (searching "COPD at 35," "why emphysema non-smoker"), Facebook (COPD groups, lung health pages)
- **Foundation's Goal**: Intercept searches, target ads ‚Üí drive to symptom checker ‚Üí get tested ‚Üí diagnosed
- **Platforms**: Google Ads (search), Facebook Ads (audience targeting), YouTube (pre-roll on COPD videos)

---

**Audience 2: Diagnosed Patients (Community Building)**

- **Who**: 10,000-20,000 diagnosed AATD patients (need support, education, connection)
- **Where They Are**: Facebook groups ("Alpha-1 Support"), Instagram (#Alpha1Warrior hashtag), patient forums
- **Foundation's Goal**: Build community ‚Üí support groups, patient navigation, advocacy ‚Üí lifelong engagement (donors, volunteers)
- **Platforms**: Facebook (groups, pages), Instagram (stories, patient spotlights), YouTube (education videos)

---

**Audience 3: Healthcare Professionals (Diagnosis Enablers)**

- **Who**: Primary care physicians, pulmonologists, hepatologists (can order testing‚Äîneed education)
- **Where They Are**: LinkedIn (professional network), Medscape (CME), medical journals (online ads)
- **Foundation's Goal**: Educate ‚Üí test more patients ‚Üí increase diagnosis ‚Üí Foundation's ultimate goal
- **Platforms**: LinkedIn Ads (target "pulmonologist," "primary care"), retargeting (website visitors‚ÄîHCP section)

---

**Audience 4: Donors (Revenue)**

- **Who**: Philanthropists, AATD families, empathetic individuals (care about rare disease‚Äîwilling to give)
- **Where They Are**: Facebook (nonprofit followers), Instagram (cause-driven), email (newsletter subscribers)
- **Foundation's Goal**: Inspire giving ‚Üí donations ‚Üí fund programs, research ‚Üí mission fulfillment
- **Platforms**: Facebook/Instagram Ads (lookalike audiences‚Äîpast donors), email campaigns (segmented), retargeting (website visitors who didn't donate)

## Platform Strategy

### Platform 1: Facebook (Community Hub)

**Why Facebook?**

- ‚úÖ **Largest user base** (2.9B users‚Äîeveryone's on Facebook, patients age 40-70 especially active)
- ‚úÖ **Groups** (private communities‚Äîpatients share experiences, support each other, Foundation moderates)
- ‚úÖ **Targeted ads** (hyper-specific‚Äîtarget "COPD," "emphysema," "lung disease," geographic, age, behaviors)
- ‚úÖ **Fundraising tools** (birthday fundraisers, donation buttons‚Äîeasy peer-to-peer, integrated)

**Foundation's Facebook Presence**:

**1. Mark Egly Foundation Page** (Public, Brand Presence)

- **Content**: Educational posts (2-3/week‚Äî"What is Alpha-1?", treatment tips, research updates), patient stories (1/week‚Äîvideo or text), event announcements (Walk, Gala, webinars)
- **Goal**: 10K followers Year 1 ‚Üí 50K followers Year 5 (broad awareness, SEO benefit [social signals])
- **Engagement Strategy**: Ask questions ("Have you been tested for Alpha-1?"), polls ("Which symptom brought you to diagnosis?"), encourage comments (respond within 24 hours‚Äîbuild relationships)

**2. Alpha-1 Community Group** (Private, Patient Support)

- **Content**: Peer support (patients post questions‚Äî"Just diagnosed, scared, advice?"), Foundation staff moderate (answer questions, share resources, no misinformation), weekly themes (Monday Motivation [inspiring quotes], Wednesday Wisdom [medical info], Friday Spotlight [member story])
- **Goal**: 500 members Year 1 ‚Üí 5,000 members Year 5 (largest private AATD community‚Äîcompetitive with existing groups)
- **Moderation**: 3 rules (1. Be kind/supportive‚Äîno negativity, 2. No medical advice‚Äîencourage doctor consultations, 3. Privacy‚Äîdon't share others' info), Foundation staff monitors daily (remove spam, answer questions)

**Facebook Ads Strategy**:

- **Campaign 1: Awareness** (Target: Undiagnosed patients, Budget: $2K/month = $24K/year)
  - Audience: Age 30-65, interests: COPD, emphysema, lung disease, liver disease, genetic testing
  - Ad creative: "Have COPD but never smoked? It could be Alpha-1. Take our 2-minute quiz." (link to symptom checker)
  - Expected results: 500K impressions/month, 5K clicks (1% CTR), 500 symptom checker completions (10% conversion), 75 high-risk (15%)‚Äî$24K / 900 high-risk leads/year = $27/lead (cost-effective)

- **Campaign 2: Retargeting** (Target: Website visitors who didn't convert, Budget: $500/month = $6K/year)
  - Audience: Visited Foundation website (read articles, watched videos‚Äîengaged but didn't join group, donate, or find specialist)
  - Ad creative: "Come back! Join our community of Alpha-1 warriors" (link to Facebook group), "Find a specialist near you" (link to directory)
  - Expected results: 50K impressions/month, 1K clicks (2% CTR‚Äîwarm audience), 100 conversions (10%‚Äîsupport group signups, specialist searches)

- **Campaign 3: Fundraising** (Target: Lookalike donors, Budget: $1K/month = $12K/year)
  - Audience: Lookalike of past donors (Facebook finds similar users‚Äîdemographics, interests, behaviors match Foundation's donor profile)
  - Ad creative: Patient testimonial video ("Alpha-1 gave me emphysema at 38. The Foundation saved my life. Please donate."), donate button
  - Expected results: 100K impressions/month, 2K clicks (2% CTR), 60 donations (3% conversion, $100 avg.) = $6K/month revenue ($72K/year revenue from $12K ad spend = 6:1 ROI‚Äîexcellent)

**Total Facebook Ads Budget**: $42K/year (Year 1)
