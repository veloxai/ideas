## On-Page SEO

### Page Optimization Checklist (Every Article)

**1. Title Tag** (50-60 characters, include primary keyword):

- ‚úÖ Good: "Alpha-1 Antitrypsin Deficiency: Symptoms, Causes, Treatment" (keyword front-loaded, clear, 58 chars)
- ‚ùå Bad: "Understanding a Rare Genetic Condition That Affects Lungs" (no keyword, vague, 64 chars)

**2. Meta Description** (150-160 characters, compelling + keyword):

- ‚úÖ Good: "Alpha-1 antitrypsin deficiency (AATD) is a genetic disorder causing lung and liver disease. Learn symptoms, testing, treatment options from the Mark Egly Foundation." (159 chars, keyword, value prop)
- ‚ùå Bad: "Learn about this condition." (too short, no keyword, no value)

**3. H1 Heading** (1 per page, primary keyword):

- ‚úÖ "What is Alpha-1 Antitrypsin Deficiency (AATD)?"
- ‚ùå "A Rare Genetic Disorder" (no keyword)

**4. H2/H3 Subheadings** (use secondary keywords):

- H2: "AATD Symptoms: Lung and Liver Manifestations" (keyword: "AATD symptoms")
- H3: "Early Signs of Alpha-1 Lung Disease" (keyword: "early signs alpha-1")

**5. Keyword Density** (1-2%‚Äînatural, not stuffed):

- 2,000-word article ‚Üí use "alpha-1" or "AATD" 20-40 times (including variations, synonyms‚Äîdon't repeat exactly)

**6. Internal Links** (3-5 per article):

- Link to related Foundation pages ("Learn more about augmentation therapy" ‚Üí Treatment page, "Find a specialist" ‚Üí Directory)
- **Why**: Keeps users on site (reduces bounce rate), distributes link equity (improves other pages' SEO)

**7. External Links** (2-3 authoritative sources):

- Cite studies (NEJM, AJRCCM), guidelines (ATS/ERS), government (NIH, FDA)
- **Why**: Google rewards citing sources (trustworthiness signal), users appreciate references

**8. Images** (3-5 per article, optimized):

- Alt text with keywords ("Alpha-1 patient receiving augmentation therapy infusion"‚Äîdescriptive + keyword)
- File names ("alpha-1-infusion.jpg"‚Äînot "IMG_1234.jpg")
- Compressed (<100KB‚Äîfast load, don't sacrifice quality)

**9. CTA (Call-to-Action)** (1-2 per article):

- "Find a specialist near you" (button ‚Üí directory)
- "Join a support group" (button ‚Üí support group finder)
- "Donate to fund research" (button ‚Üí donation page)
- **Why**: Convert readers to engaged users (SEO brings them in, CTA converts)

**10. Schema Markup** (structured data):

- Article schema (title, author, date, image‚ÄîGoogle shows in search results)
- Organization schema (Foundation name, logo, address‚ÄîKnowledge Graph)
- FAQ schema (if article has FAQs‚ÄîGoogle shows accordion in results, increases CTR)

## Technical SEO

### Site-Wide Optimizations

**1. Site Speed** (Target: <2 seconds load time)

- **Why**: Google ranking factor (2018 update), users abandon slow sites (53% bounce if >3 seconds)
- **How**:
  - Image compression (Smush plugin, WebP format‚Äî50-80% smaller)
  - Caching (WP Rocket, browser cache‚Äîreturn visitors instant load)
  - CDN (Cloudflare, $20/month‚Äîserve images from nearest server, global fast)
  - Lazy loading (images load as user scrolls‚Äîabove-fold instant)

**Current Speed Test**: GTmetrix, Google PageSpeed Insights (aim 90+ score, Year 1 80-85, optimize to 90+ Year 2)

---

**2. Mobile-Friendliness** (65% of searches mobile)

- Responsive design (WordPress theme mobile-optimized‚Äîalready covered in Website doc)
- Large tap targets (buttons 44px minimum‚Äîavoid tiny links)
- Fast mobile load (3G networks slow‚Äîextra image compression for mobile)

**Test**: Google Mobile-Friendly Test (must pass‚ÄîGoogle mobile-first indexing, mobile version = primary)

---

**3. HTTPS** (Security = Ranking Factor)

- SSL certificate (Let's Encrypt free, or Cloudflare‚Äîalready included in WP Engine hosting)
- All links https:// (no mixed content‚Äîavoid "Not Secure" browser warning)

---

**4. XML Sitemap** (Help Google Find All Pages)

- Yoast SEO auto-generates (MarkEglyFoundation.org/sitemap.xml)
- Submit to Google Search Console (tell Google to crawl‚Äîindex faster)

---

**5. Robots.txt** (Control Crawling)

- Allow all content (Foundation wants everything indexed‚Äîno restrictions)
- Block admin pages (wp-admin‚Äîno SEO value, don't waste Google's crawl budget)

---

**6. Canonical Tags** (Avoid Duplicate Content)

- Each page points to itself as canonical (Yoast handles automatically)
- If syndicate content (Foundation article republished on medical site‚Äîcanonical back to Foundation, Foundation gets SEO credit)

---

**7. 404 Error Handling** (Fix Broken Links)

- Custom 404 page ("Page not found, here are helpful links"‚Äîhomepage, search, contact)
- Monitor broken links (Google Search Console‚Äîfix immediately, don't lose link equity)
