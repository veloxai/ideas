## Technology & Infrastructure

### Donation Processing

**Options**:

**1. Classy or Givebutter** (Online fundraising platform):

- Embed donation form (on Foundation websiteâ€”company-specific landing page, track campaign separately)
- Accept one-time + recurring (payroll deduction = recurring monthly donation processed via credit card)
- Matching gift integration (Classy partners with Double the Donationâ€”auto-detects employer, prompts match submission)
- Cost: 2.9% + $0.30 per transaction (credit card feesâ€”standard)

**2. Direct Payroll Deduction** (through company HR):

- Company deducts from paycheck (sends lump sum to Foundationâ€”monthly or quarterly)
- No transaction fees (ACH transferâ€”Foundation receives 100%)
- Preferred for large campaigns (high volumeâ€”transaction fees add up, direct payroll more efficient)

**Recommendation**: Hybrid approach

- Small companies (<500 employees): Use Classy (easy setup, handles everything)
- Large companies (500+ employees): Direct payroll deduction (negotiate with HRâ€”lower fees, higher net revenue)

---

### Matching Gift Automation

**Problem**: Many matching gifts go unclaimed (employees donate, forget to submit matchâ€”30-40% of eligible matches never processed)

**Solution**: Double the Donation (or 360MatchPro)

- Integrates with donation platform (when employee donates, tool auto-detects employer from email domain)
- Prompts employee ("Your employer offers matching gifts! Click here to submit your match request.")
- Increases match capture 60-80% (from 30% to 60-70%â€”doubles matching revenue)
- Cost: $1,000-$3,000/year (subscriptionâ€”worth it, pays for itself in increased matches)

**Expected Impact**:

- Without tool: $100K in donations â†’ $30K in matches (30% capture rate)
- With tool: $100K in donations â†’ $60K in matches (60% capture rate)
- Net gain: $30K additional revenue (minus $2K tool cost = $28K ROI)
