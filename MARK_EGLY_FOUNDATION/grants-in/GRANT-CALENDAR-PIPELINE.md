- Review grant tracking database (upcoming deadlines, applications in progress)

## Pipeline Management System

### Pipeline Stages

**Stage 1: Identified** (200-300 grants/year)

- Grant opportunity discovered
- Added to database with basic info
- Not yet evaluated

**Stage 2: Qualified** (100-150 grants/year)

- Reviewed against fit criteria
- Meets basic eligibility
- Worth pursuing

**Stage 3: In Development** (30-50 grants/year)

- Actively writing proposal
- Gathering materials
- Assigned to grant writer

**Stage 4: Submitted** (20-40 grants/year)

- Application submitted by deadline
- Awaiting decision
- Following up with program officer

**Stage 5: Under Review** (10-30 grants at any time)

- Funder reviewing application
- May request additional info
- Decision pending

**Stage 6: Awarded** (10-20 grants/year by Year 3)

- Grant approved!
- Contract negotiation
- Move to Grant Management

**Stage 7: Declined** (10-20 grants/year)

- Application not funded
- Request feedback
- Update lessons learned

---

### Pipeline Health Metrics

**Healthy Pipeline Indicators**:

- ‚úÖ 3-5x submitted applications vs. funding goal (e.g., $500K goal ‚Üí $1.5-2.5M in applications)
- ‚úÖ Mix of small ($10-50K), medium ($50-150K), large ($150K+) grants
- ‚úÖ Mix of short-term (3-6 month decision) and long-term (6-12 month) grants
- ‚úÖ No single funder >30% of total pipeline (diversification)
- ‚úÖ 30-40% win rate (if lower, strategy needs adjustment)

**Unhealthy Pipeline Indicators**:

- ‚ùå Too few applications (<10/year) ‚Üí won't hit funding goal
- ‚ùå Too many applications (>50/year) ‚Üí quality suffering, burnout
- ‚ùå Only large grants (high risk‚Äîif lose, big funding gap)
- ‚ùå Only small grants (can't scale)
- ‚ùå Win rate <20% (applying to wrong grants or weak proposals)

## Success Tracking

### Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

**Monthly**:

- Opportunities identified
- Applications submitted
- Decisions received (win/loss)

**Quarterly**:

- Funding secured vs. goal
- Win rate (% of applications funded)
- Pipeline value (total $ in submitted applications)

**Annually**:

- Total funding secured
- Number of grants awarded
- Funding by source (foundation, government, corporate)
- Funding by program area (research, education, patient services)

---

### Dashboard Metrics (Track in Grant Database)

**Pipeline Metrics**:

- Active opportunities (Identified, Qualified, In Development)
- Applications submitted (Submitted, Under Review)
- Pending decisions (# and $ value)
- Conversion rates by stage

**Performance Metrics**:

- YTD funding secured vs. annual goal (e.g., $150K / $300K = 50%)
- Win rate (YTD and trailing 12 months)
- Average grant size
- Time from submission to decision (by funder type)

**Portfolio Metrics**:

- Active grants (awarded, in progress)
- Reporting due dates (upcoming 30/60/90 days)
- Concentration risk (% from top 3 funders)
