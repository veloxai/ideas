## Stewardship Cycle

### 7-Touch Stewardship Cycle (Annual Donors)

**Cycle**: Repeat every 12 months for all donors

**Touch 1: Thank You (Within 48 Hours)**:

- **Who**: All donors
- **Channel**: Email (automated) + personal call or handwritten note (Major Donors)
- **Message**: "Thank you for your gift of $[Amount]. Your generosity will [specific impact]."

**Touch 2: Tax Receipt (Within 1 Week)**:

- **Who**: All donors
- **Channel**: Email (automated) or mail
- **Message**: Official tax receipt (IRS compliance)

**Touch 3: Impact Update (30 Days After Gift)**:

- **Who**: All donors
- **Channel**: Email (segmented by donor level)
- **Message**: "Here's what your gift accomplished: [specific outcomes, stories, data]"

**Touch 4: Invitation to Event (60-90 Days)**:

- **Who**: Mid-level + Major Donors (all invited), Small Donors (select few)
- **Channel**: Email + personal call (Major Donors)
- **Message**: "Join us for [golf tournament, patient symposium, gala, etc.]"

**Touch 5: Quarterly Newsletter (Every 3 Months)**:

- **Who**: All donors
- **Channel**: Email (with print option for Major Donors)
- **Message**: Foundation updates, patient stories, research news, advocacy wins

**Touch 6: Annual Report (12 Months After Gift)**:

- **Who**: All donors ($100+)
- **Channel**: Digital (email) or print (Major Donors)
- **Message**: Year in reviewâ€”impact, financials, donor recognition

**Touch 7: Renewal Ask (13-14 Months After Last Gift)**:

- **Who**: All donors
- **Channel**: Multi-channel (email, mail, phone for Major Donors)
- **Message**: "Will you renew your support? Your gift of $[last year's amount + 10-20%] will [impact]."

**Cycle Repeats**: After renewal, restart at Touch 1

---

### Major Donor Stewardship Cycle (Additional Touches)

**Major Donors ($10K+) = 12-15 Touches/Year**:

In addition to the 7 annual touches above, add:

**Touch 8: Personal Meeting (In-Person or Zoom)**:

- **When**: 30-60 days after gift
- **Who**: CEO or Chief Development Officer
- **Purpose**: Build relationship, understand donor's interests, explore deeper involvement

**Touch 9: Behind-the-Scenes Tour/Experience**:

- **When**: 3-6 months after gift
- **Examples**:
  - Lab tour (see AAT glucometer development)
  - Patient panel (meet AATD patients)
  - Board meeting (attend as observer)
  - Research symposium (meet researchers)

**Touch 10: Birthday/Anniversary Card**:

- **When**: Donor's birthday or gift anniversary
- **Channel**: Handwritten card from CEO or Board Chair
- **Message**: Personal note (not ask)

**Touch 11: Special Project Update**:

- **When**: Quarterly (if donor funded specific projectâ€”e.g., research grant)
- **Channel**: Email or personal call
- **Message**: "Your grant is making a differenceâ€”here's the latest..."

**Touch 12: Exclusive Briefing**:

- **When**: Semi-annually
- **Channel**: Private Zoom call or in-person
- **Purpose**: Major donors-only briefing with CEO on Foundation strategy, challenges, opportunities

**Touch 13: Peer Networking Event**:

- **When**: Annually
- **Purpose**: Connect major donors to each other (builds community, cross-pollination of ideas)

**Touch 14: Holiday Greeting**:

- **When**: December
- **Channel**: Handwritten card from CEO
- **Message**: Season's greetings + gratitude (not ask)

**Touch 15: Thank You Video**:

- **When**: After major accomplishment (e.g., "We hit 1,000 diagnoses!")
- **Channel**: Personalized video from CEO or patient
- **Message**: "None of this would be possible without you."

### Stewardship Tactics

**1. Personal Meetings**:

- **Frequency**: 2-4x/year (in-person preferred, Zoom if distance)

- **Who**: CEO, Chief Development Officer, or Board member
- **Prep**:
  - Research donor (background, interests, past giving)

- **Phone Calls**: Quarterly check-ins (not asksâ€”just relationship-building)
- **Personal Emails**: From CEO (not mass emailsâ€”1:1 updates)

- **Custom Impact Reports**: Show specific impact of donor's gift (e.g., "Your $50K funded 500 AATD testsâ€”here are the 25 patients diagnosed")
- **Quarterly Updates**: Email or printed booklet with photos, patient stories, data

**Industry Benchmark**: Average donor retention rate = 45% (more than half of donors don't give again!)

**Mark Egly Foundation Goal**: 75%+ retention (Year 1) â†’ 85% (Year 5)

**Why It Matters**:

- **Cost**: Acquiring new donor = 5x more expensive than retaining existing donor
- **Lifetime Value**: Retained donors give more over time (increase gifts, refer others, leave planned gifts)
- **Loyalty**: Long-term donors become advocates, volunteers, ambassadors

- Goal: 75% (Year 1) â†’ 85% (Year 5)

- Formula: (# first-time donors who made second gift) / (# first-time donors)
