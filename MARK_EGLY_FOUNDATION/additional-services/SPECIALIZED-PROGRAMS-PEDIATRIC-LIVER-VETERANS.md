## Liver Disease Program

### Target Population

**Who We Serve**:

- AATD patients with liver involvement (any ageâ€”pediatric or adult)
- **Neonatal cholestasis** (ZZ infantsâ€”10% have liver disease at birth)
- **Chronic liver disease** (5-10% of ZZ adultsâ€”cirrhosis, portal hypertension)

**Liver Disease in AATD**:

- **Cause**: Z protein accumulates in liver (toxicâ€”causes inflammation, fibrosis, cirrhosis)
- **No medical treatment**: Augmentation therapy doesn't help liver (only replaces protein in bloodâ€”doesn't remove protein from liver)
- **Only cure**: Liver transplant (for cirrhosis, liver failure)

### Services Offered

#### 1. Hepatologist Network

**Problem**: Many AATD patients see pulmonologists only (liver disease missed, not monitored)

**Solution**: Foundation recruits hepatologists (liver specialists) with AATD expertise

**Network** (Year 1â€”Target 30 Hepatologists):

- Navigator provides warm referral (calls hepatologist, explains case, schedules appointment)

#### 2. Liver Screening Protocols

**Guidelines**: All AATD patients should have liver screening (many don'tâ€”doctors focus on lungs, forget liver)

**Foundation Screening Recommendations**:

- **For ZZ, SZ patients** (all ages):
  - Annual liver enzyme panel (AST, ALT, alkaline phosphatase, bilirubin, albumin, INR)
  - Ultrasound every 3-5 years (screen for cirrhosis, liver cancer)
  - FibroScan every 2-3 years (non-invasiveâ€”measures liver stiffness, detects fibrosis/cirrhosis)
- **For MZ, MS patients** (lower risk, but still monitor):
  - Liver enzymes every 2-3 years (if abnormal, escalate to annual)

**Foundation Provides**:

- **Screening Tracker** (in Medical Records Portalâ€”reminders when tests due)
- **Doctor Letter** (patient brings to PCPâ€”"Please order liver screening per Foundation guidelines")

#### 3. Liver Transplant Navigation

**Transplant Indications**:

- Cirrhosis with complications (ascites, variceal bleeding, encephalopathy)
- Liver failure (MELD score > 15)

**Foundation Transplant Support**:

- **Transplant Center Referrals**: Navigate to top centers (UCSF, Cleveland Clinic, Mayoâ€”best outcomes for AATD liver transplants)
- **Evaluation Assistance**: Help complete workup (cardiology, nephrology, psych, dental clearanceâ€”complex process)
- **Waitlist Support**: Patient Navigator checks in monthly (emotional support, answer questions, coordinate care)
- **Travel Assistance**: Foundation travel grants (cover flights, lodging for evaluation, surgery, follow-upâ€”see Medical Travel Assistance Program)
- **Post-Transplant**: Peer support groups (liver transplant survivorsâ€”share experiences, recovery tips)

#### 2. Service-Connected Disability Claims
