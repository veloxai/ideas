## Why Expert Witnesses Matter

### Medical Complexity Requires Expert Testimony

**AATD is Rare & Misunderstood**:

- Judges, juries, opposing attorneys, insurance adjusters **don't understand AATD** (never heard of it, don't know genetics, prognosis, treatment)
- **Without expert testimony**, patient's case fails (e.g., disability judge doesn't believe AATD is disablingâ€”denies claim)
- **With expert testimony**, judge/jury understands (expert explains disease, limitations, why patient can't workâ€”case succeeds)

**Experts Establish**:

- **Standard of care** (what reasonable doctor should doâ€”did defendant doctor meet standard? if not, malpractice)
- **Causation** (did doctor's negligence cause harm? or was outcome inevitable given AATD progression?)
- **Disability** (can patient work? what limitations? what accommodations needed?)
- **Prognosis** (life expectancy, future medical needsâ€”for damages calculation)
- **Genetic implications** (inheritance, family testing, prenatal diagnosisâ€”for custody, family law cases)

## Legal Scenarios Requiring Experts

### 1. Medical Malpractice

**Common Claims**:
