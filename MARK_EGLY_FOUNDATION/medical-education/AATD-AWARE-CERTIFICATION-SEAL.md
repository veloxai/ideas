## Program Overview

### Concept: "AATD Aware" Certification

**Inspired by**: Fair Underwriting certification in life insurance industry

- Life insurance agents who complete AATD education receive "Fair Underwriting" certification
- Certified agents commit to not discriminating against AATD applicants
- Certification is recognized industry-wide and builds trust with AATD community

**Our Model**: "AATD Aware" certification for physicians

- Physicians complete CME course + demonstrate commitment to early AATD testing
- Receive certification seal to display on website, office, marketing materials
- Listed in foundation's online provider directory
- Recognized by AATD patients as "safe" providers who understand the condition

---

**For Physicians**:

- ‚úÖ Demonstrates expertise in AATD (competitive advantage)

## Certification Requirements

### Tier 1: AATD Aware Certified (Basic)

**Who**: All physicians (any specialty)

**Requirements**:

1. ‚úÖ Complete foundational CME course (1.0 credits): "AATD: Early Detection, Better Outcomes"
2. ‚úÖ Pass post-test (70%+ score)
3. ‚úÖ Sign commitment pledge (see below)
4. ‚úÖ Maintain active medical license (MD/DO)
5. ‚úÖ Pay certification fee: **$0** (Year 1-2), **$99/year** (Year 3+)

**Commitment Pledge**:
_"As an AATD Aware Certified physician, I commit to:_

- _Test all COPD patients for AATD per WHO guidelines_
- _Consider AATD testing for patients with unexplained lung disease, early-onset emphysema, or family history_
- _Refer AATD patients to specialists when appropriate_
- _Provide genetic counseling and recommend family testing_
- _Stay current on AATD diagnosis and management"_

**Certification Valid**: 2 years

---

### Tier 2: AATD Specialist Certified (Advanced)

**Who**: Pulmonologists, geneticists, hepatologists with AATD expertise

**Requirements**:

1. ‚úÖ Complete all requirements for Tier 1
2. ‚úÖ Complete advanced CME course: "Advanced AATD Management" (2.0 credits)
3. ‚úÖ Demonstrate AATD clinical experience:
   - Managed 10+ AATD patients OR
   - Prescribed augmentation therapy OR
   - Conducted AATD research (published)
4. ‚úÖ Provide 2 professional references (colleagues or patients)
5. ‚úÖ Pay certification fee: **$0** (Year 1-2), **$199/year** (Year 3+)

**Commitment Pledge** (same as Tier 1 + additional):

- _Accept referrals from AATD Aware Certified physicians_
- _Participate in foundation initiatives (education, advocacy, research)_
- _Mentor other physicians on AATD diagnosis/management_

**Certification Valid**: 3 years

---

### Tier 3: AATD Center of Excellence (Institutional)

**Who**: Medical centers, hospitals, or multi-physician practices

**Requirements**:

1. ‚úÖ Minimum 3 AATD Specialist Certified physicians on staff
2. ‚úÖ Institutional commitment to AATD standards:
   - AATD testing protocol in place (EMR order sets, algorithms)
   - Dedicated AATD clinic or program
   - Multidisciplinary team (pulmonology, genetics, hepatology)
3. ‚úÖ Quality metrics:
   - Test 90%+ of COPD patients for AATD
   - Diagnostic turnaround time <30 days
   - Patient satisfaction >85%
4. ‚úÖ Participate in foundation data collection (anonymized outcomes)
5. ‚úÖ Pay certification fee: **$0** (Year 1-3), **$2,500/year** (Year 4+)

**Benefits**:

- Listed as Center of Excellence in provider directory
- Priority referrals from foundation
- Collaboration on research and clinical trials
- Featured in foundation marketing materials

**Certification Valid**: 3 years (with annual quality reporting)

## Application Process

### Step 1: Complete CME Course

Physician completes foundational CME course (or advanced for Tier 2)

- Online, self-paced
- Pass post-test (70%+)

---

### Step 2: Apply for Certification

**Online Application Form** (on foundation website):

- Personal Information: Name, credentials, NPI number, medical license number
- Practice Information: Specialty, office address, phone, website
- CME Completion: Certificate of completion (auto-populated if taken our course)
- Commitment Pledge: Check box "I agree to AATD Aware Commitment Pledge"
- References (Tier 2 only): 2 professional references (name, email, relationship)
- Payment: Credit card (if applicable; free Year 1-2)

**Institutional Application** (Tier 3):

- Institutional contact person
- List of AATD Specialist Certified physicians on staff
- Description of AATD program/clinic
- Quality metrics data (testing rates, turnaround time, satisfaction)

---

### Step 3: Review & Approval

**Review Process**:

- Foundation staff reviews application (1-3 business days)
- Verify medical license (active, no sanctions)
- Verify CME completion
- Check references (Tier 2 only)

**Approval**:

- Applicant receives email: "Congratulations! You are now AATD Aware Certified."
- Certificate and seal emailed as PDF and digital files
- Added to provider directory within 24 hours

**Denial** (rare):

- If license is inactive or sanctioned
- If failed to complete CME course
- If pledge conflicts with practice patterns (e.g., physician publicly opposes AATD testing)

---

### Step 4: Onboarding

**Welcome Package** (email):

- Digital certificate (PDF)
- Certification seal (PNG, SVG, various sizes)
- Marketing toolkit:
  - Sample website language: "I am AATD Aware Certified by Mark Egly Foundation"
  - Sample social media post: "Proud to be AATD Aware Certified!"
  - Sample patient brochure: "This practice is AATD Aware"
- Provider directory profile link
- AATD resource library (guidelines, patient handouts, coding/billing info)

**Optional**: Welcome call from foundation staff (15 minutes)

- Answer questions
- Discuss ways to get involved (advocacy, research, education)

## Quality Assurance

### Maintaining Standards

**Annual Survey** (sent to all certified physicians):

- How many AATD tests have you ordered this year?
- How many patients diagnosed with AATD?
- Are you following the AATD Aware Commitment Pledge?
- What additional resources do you need?

**Purpose**: Ensure certified physicians are actively testing for AATD (not just displaying seal)

---

### Patient Feedback

**Optional Patient Reviews** (Year 3+):

- Patients can rate certified physicians (1-5 stars)
- Comment on AATD-specific care: "Did physician explain AATD clearly?" "Was genetic counseling provided?"
- Displayed on provider directory (if physician opts in)

**Purpose**: Hold certified physicians accountable to patient-centered care

---

### Revocation of Certification

**Grounds for Revocation** (rare):

- License suspended or revoked
- Malpractice claim related to AATD diagnostic delay
- Patient complaints about failure to test or poor care
- Violation of AATD Aware Commitment Pledge
- Failure to renew (expired certification)

**Process**:

1. Foundation investigates complaint or concern
2. Physician given opportunity to respond
3. If revocation warranted, physician notified in writing
4. Removed from provider directory
5. Must cease using AATD Aware seal

**Appeals Process**: Physician can appeal revocation to Board of Directors

## Renewal & Recertification

### Tier 1: Renewal Every 2 Years

**Requirements**:

- ‚úÖ Maintain active medical license
- ‚úÖ Complete 1.0 CME credits in AATD (can retake original course or take new course)
- ‚úÖ Reaffirm commitment pledge
- ‚úÖ Complete annual survey (testing data)
- ‚úÖ Pay renewal fee: **$0** (Year 1-2), **$99** (Year 3+)

**Process**:

- Foundation sends renewal reminder 60 days before expiration
- Physician completes renewal application (online, 5 minutes)
- New certificate issued (updated year)

---

### Tier 2: Renewal Every 3 Years

**Requirements**:

- ‚úÖ All Tier 1 renewal requirements
- ‚úÖ Complete 2.0 additional CME credits in AATD
- ‚úÖ Demonstrate continued AATD clinical experience (10+ patients in last 3 years)
- ‚úÖ Pay renewal fee: **$0** (Year 1-2), **$199** (Year 3+)

---

### Tier 3: Renewal Every 3 Years

**Requirements**:

- ‚úÖ Maintain 3+ AATD Specialist Certified physicians on staff
- ‚úÖ Submit annual quality metrics reports
- ‚úÖ Demonstrate continued commitment to AATD standards
- ‚úÖ Pay renewal fee: **$0** (Year 1-3), **$2,500** (Year 4+)

---

### Lapsed Certification

**Grace Period**: 30 days after expiration

- Physician can renew without penalty

**After Grace Period**:

- Certification revoked
- Removed from provider directory
- Must reapply (not renewal) to regain certification
