## Platform Strategy

### Strategic Pillars

**1. Safety First**:

- Moderated community (prevent misinformation, bullying, harassment)
- Private profiles (members control what they share)
- HIPAA-compliant (no PHI shared publicly without consent)

**2. Patient-Centered Design**:

- Mobile-first (patients access on phonesâ€”during infusions, doctor visits, bedtime)
- Accessibility (WCAG 2.1 AAâ€”screen readers, colorblind-friendly, simple language)
- Low-barrier entry (no medical jargon, welcoming tone, easy registration)

**3. Content Over Features**:

- Start simple (stories, forums, resources)
- Add features based on member requests (don't over-engineer Year 1)

**4. Community-Led**:

- Members create content (not just Foundation staff)
- Patient moderators (compensatedâ€”peer moderation builds trust)
- Member spotlight program (celebrate patients who contribute)
