### Vetting Process for Pulmonologists

**Step 1: Initial Submission**

- Provider submits online form OR patient/colleague nominates
- Form collects:
  - Name, credentials (MD, DO)
  - Practice name, address, phone
  - Board certification info
  - Number of AATD patients treated
  - Augmentation therapy experience
  - References (2 peers OR 3 patient testimonials)

**Step 2: Verification**

- âœ… Verify medical license (state medical board website)
- âœ… Verify board certification (American Board of Internal Medicine)
- âœ… Check for disciplinary actions (state board, NPDB if accessible)
- âœ… Confirm practice location (Google Maps, practice website)

**Step 3: Reference Check**

- Contact 2 peer references (other MDs, AATD specialists)
- OR review patient testimonials (minimum 3, from different patients)
- Questions:
  - How long have you known/worked with this provider?
  - How many AATD patients do they treat?
  - Would you refer your own family member with AATD to this provider?
  - Any concerns we should know about?

**Step 4: Alpha-1 Foundation Cross-Check**

- Check if provider is in Alpha-1 Foundation Clinical Resource Center directory
- If yes, automatic approval (Foundation already vetted)

**Step 5: Approval Decision**

- Medical Advisory Board member reviews application
- Decision: Approve, Request More Info, or Decline
- If approved, provider added to directory within 5 business days

**Step 6: Annual Re-Verification**

- Every 12 months, verify:
  - Still practicing at listed location
  - License still active
  - No new disciplinary actions
  - Still treating AATD patients

## ðŸ” Quality Assurance

### Annual Re-Verification (All Providers)

**Each Year** (on anniversary of listing):

- Email provider: "Please confirm you're still practicing and accepting AATD patients"
- Re-verify license (state board website)
- Check for new disciplinary actions
- Update any changed information (address, phone, etc.)

**If provider doesn't respond within 30 days**:

- Mark listing as "Unverified" (hide from public view)
- Send 2nd email reminder
- If still no response after 60 days, remove from directory

---

### Patient Feedback Mechanism

**Reporting Issues**:

- Patients can report problems with providers (inaccurate info, poor experience, provider no longer practices)
- Form: "Report an Issue with This Listing"
- Staff investigates within 5 business days
- If multiple reports or serious issue, remove listing pending investigation

**Positive Feedback** (Future):

- Consider adding patient reviews/ratings (similar to Healthgrades)
- Must be verified patients (require Alpha1Life account)
- Moderated (no fake reviews, inflammatory content)
