## ğŸ‘¥ Donor Segmentation

### Segment 1: **AATD Patients** (Primary)

**Characteristics**:

- Diagnosed with Alpha-1
- Ages 35-70
- Middle to upper-middle income
- Grateful for resources/support

**Motivations**:

- Personal connection to cause
- Want to help others avoid their struggles
- Desire to fund research for better treatments
- Community belonging

**Giving Pattern**:

- Lower average gift ($25-$50)
- Higher lifetime value (recurring)
- Emotionally engaged

**Messaging**:

- "You found support here. Help others find it too."
- "Your gift helps newly diagnosed patients like you once were."
- Peer-to-peer: "I'm fundraising for the community that helped me."

---

### Segment 2: **Family Members** (Secondary)

**Characteristics**:

- Spouse, parent, child, sibling of patient
- Ages 25-65
- Emotionally invested in cure/support

**Motivations**:

- Love for affected family member
- Desire to prevent suffering
- Guilt relief (if felt helpless)
- Hope for better future

**Giving Pattern**:

- Moderate gifts ($50-$100)
- Memorial/honor giving
- Event participation

**Messaging**:

- "In honor of [loved one's name]"
- "Help families like mine find answers"
- "Every family deserves support"

---

### Segment 3: **Healthcare Providers** (Niche)

**Characteristics**:

- Pulmonologists, hepatologists, genetic counselors
- Treat AATD patients
- High income

**Motivations**:

- Professional interest in better outcomes
- Want resources for patients
- Research advancement

**Giving Pattern**:

- Larger gifts ($100-$500)
- Infrequent (annual)
- Appreciate professionalism

**Messaging**:

- "Support resources your patients need"
- "Advance AATD research and education"
- "Help close gaps in patient care"

---

### Segment 4: **Grateful Patients** (High-Value)

**Characteristics**:

- Long-term survivors
- Received significant benefit from treatment/support
- Financially stable

**Motivations**:

- Gratitude for life
- Desire to "give back"
- Legacy building

**Giving Pattern**:

- Major gifts ($1,000+)
- Planned giving
- Lifetime donors

**Messaging**:

- "You beat the odds. Help others do the same."
- "Your legacy: A world where Alpha-1 is manageable"
- "Join our leadership circle"

---

### Segment 5: **General Supporters** (Broad)

**Characteristics**:

- No direct connection to AATD
- Interested in rare diseases, lung health, or healthcare broadly
- Found site through search/social

**Motivations**:

- Altruism
- Tax deduction
- Emotional appeal of patient stories

**Giving Pattern**:

- One-time gifts ($10-$25)
- Lower retention
- Responsive to urgent appeals

**Messaging**:

- "Every donation helps rare disease patients"
- "A gift of any size makes an impact"
- Emotional storytelling

### A/B Testing Ideas

**Test 1: Suggested Amounts**

- **Version A**: $25, $50, $100, $250
- **Version B**: $50, $100, $250, $500 (higher anchor)
- **Hypothesis**: Higher anchors increase average gift

**Test 2: Default Frequency**

- **Version A**: One-time selected by default
- **Version B**: Monthly selected by default
- **Hypothesis**: Default to monthly increases recurring donors

**Test 3: Impact Descriptions**

- **Version A**: Specific impact ("Provides educational packet to 1 patient")
- **Version B**: Emotional impact ("Gives hope to a scared newly diagnosed patient")
- **Hypothesis**: Emotional language increases donations

**Test 4: Video Placement**

- **Version A**: Video at top of page
- **Version B**: Video after donation form (reduce distraction)
- **Hypothesis**: Video after form increases completion

**Test 5: Processing Fee Coverage**

- **Version A**: Checkbox to cover fees (opt-in)
- **Version B**: Fees covered by default (opt-out)
- **Hypothesis**: Default opt-in increases fee coverage

## ğŸ”„ Recurring Giving Program

### "Alpha-1 Champions" Monthly Giving Circle

**Program Name**: Alpha-1 Champions (or "Breath Partners", "Hope Circle")

**Tagline**: "A small monthly gift. A lifetime of impact."

---

### Benefits of Monthly Giving

**For MEF**:

- Predictable revenue
- Higher lifetime value (12x annual gifts)
- Lower cost per dollar raised
- Better cash flow

**For Donors**:

- Affordable ($10-$20/month vs. $120-$240 lump sum)
- Automatic (set it and forget it)
- Tax receipt at year-end
- Exclusive updates and impact stories

---

### Monthly Giving Tiers

**Tier 1: Supporter** - $10/month ($120/year)

- Quarterly email updates
- Annual impact report
- Member badge on forum profile (if applicable)

**Tier 2: Advocate** - $25/month ($300/year)

- All Supporter benefits
- Exclusive monthly video updates from MEF leadership
- Early access to events

**Tier 3: Champion** - $50/month ($600/year)

- All Advocate benefits
- Annual thank-you call from MEF founder
- Name listed on website (optional)
- Invitation to annual donor appreciation event

**Tier 4: Hero** - $100+/month ($1,200+/year)

- All Champion benefits
- Quarterly calls with medical advisory board
- Personalized impact report
- Special recognition

---

### Recruitment Strategy

**On Donation Page**:

- Show monthly amount vs. annual: "$25/month = $300/year impact"
- Highlight: "Join 127 Alpha-1 Champions giving monthly"
- Offer incentive: "Become a monthly donor and receive exclusive updates"

**Email Campaign** (to one-time donors):

- Subject: "Turn your $50 gift into $600 of impact"
- Content: Explain benefits of recurring giving, share story of monthly donor
- CTA: "Join Alpha-1 Champions"

**After First Donation**:

- Thank-you page: "Love what we do? Consider a monthly gift"
- Follow-up email (3 days later): "Your gift made an impact. Make it last."

**Testimonial Campaign**:

- Feature monthly donors on site: "Why I give monthly"
- Video testimonials from Champions

---

### Retention Strategy

**Welcome Series** (for new monthly donors):

- **Email 1** (immediate): "Welcome to Alpha-1 Champions!"
- **Email 2** (1 week): "Meet your fellow Champions" (testimonials)
- **Email 3** (1 month): "Here's the impact of your first gift"
- **Email 4** (3 months): "Thank you for 3 months of support"

**Ongoing Engagement**:

- Quarterly impact reports (specific to monthly donors)
- Monthly video messages from patients helped
- Annual "State of the Foundation" webinar (Champions-only)
- Birthday cards/emails

**Lapsed Donor Recovery**:

- If payment fails: Immediate email to update card
- If cancels: Exit survey ("Why are you leaving?")
- If cancels: Offer to reduce amount ("Can you stay at $10/month?")
- Win-back campaign (3 months after cancel): "We miss you"
