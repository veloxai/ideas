## ðŸ“¢ Marketing Channels & Tactics

### Channel 1: Organic Search (SEO)

**Strategy**: Rank #1 for AATD patient support keywords

**Target Keywords**:

- **High Priority**: "Alpha-1 support", "AATD patient community", "living with Alpha-1", "newly diagnosed Alpha-1"
- **Medium Priority**: "Alpha-1 forum", "AATD patient stories", "Alpha-1 treatment options", "Alpha-1 specialist"
- **Long-Tail**: "what to expect with Alpha-1 diagnosis", "how to tell family about AATD", "Alpha-1 augmentation therapy side effects"

**Tactics**:

- **On-Page SEO**:

  - Optimize title tags, meta descriptions, headers
  - Internal linking structure
  - Schema markup (organization, FAQ, article)
  - Alt text for images

- **Content Marketing**:

  - Publish 2-3 new blog posts per month
  - Update and refresh popular pages quarterly
  - Create ultimate guides ("Complete Guide to AATD Treatment", "AATD Diagnosis: What to Expect")

- **Off-Page SEO**:
  - Build backlinks from Alpha-1 Foundation, NORD, health websites
  - Guest posting on health blogs
  - HARO (Help a Reporter Out) for media mentions
  - Local citations (if MEF has physical location)

**Metrics**:

- Organic traffic growth: 10% month-over-month
- Top 3 Google ranking for 10+ keywords by Year 1 end
- 50% of traffic from organic search by Year 3
