## ğŸ“‹ Executive Summary

### The Problem

Living with Alpha-1 antitrypsin deficiency (AATD) profoundly impacts daily life and mobility. Patients on supplemental oxygen face complex logistics when traveling: finding portable oxygen concentrators (POCs), coordinating with airlines, locating oxygen suppliers at destinations, and navigating insurance coverage for travel equipment. Many avoid travel altogether due to these barriers, leading to social isolation and diminished quality of life.

Beyond travel, patients struggle with everyday lifestyle questions: Can I scuba dive? Is high altitude safe? What jobs accommodate oxygen use? Can I get life insurance? Which gyms are oxygen-friendly? These questions lack centralized, disease-specific answers, forcing patients to piece together information from generic disability resources or trial-and-error.

**Key Statistics**:

- **68% of Alpha-1 patients on oxygen** have avoided travel due to logistical concerns (Alpha-1 Foundation survey, 2024)
- **$2,500-$5,000** out-of-pocket cost for a single week-long trip requiring oxygen delivery (cruise, international destination)
- **40+ hours** spent researching and coordinating travel logistics per trip
- **82% report** they would travel more with better resources and support

### The Solution

Build a comprehensive **Travel & Lifestyle Portal** on Alpha1Life.com that provides:

1. **Oxygen Provider Directory**: Searchable database of 2,000+ oxygen suppliers (home delivery, POC rentals, cruise ship services) with ratings, pricing, and Alpha-1 patient reviews
2. **Travel Planning Tools**: Step-by-step guides for flying (TSA, FAA, airline policies), cruises, international travel, road trips; customizable checklists
3. **Airline Policy Database**: Up-to-date POC approval lists, advance notice requirements, and contact info for medical desks at 50+ airlines
4. **Insurance Travel Coverage Guide**: How to get insurance to cover travel oxygen, appeal denials, Medicare/Medicaid rules
5. **Lifestyle Resources**: Job accommodations, exercise programs, high-altitude safety, recreational activities (skiing, hiking, boating)
6. **Community Travel Stories**: User-generated trip reports, tips, and photos (inspire and educate)
7. **Concierge Service** (premium): Paid service where MEF staff coordinate full travel logistics for $500-$1,500 per trip

### Impact

**Year 1 Targets**:

- **5,000+ patients** use the portal (50% of diagnosed population)
- **800+ trips** facilitated (patients report using resources for trip planning)
- **$2M saved** in out-of-pocket costs (negotiated rates with oxygen suppliers, insurance appeal success)
- **60+ concierge bookings** ($30K-$90K revenue)
- **4.8/5 average satisfaction** rating

**Long-Term Impact**:

- Reduced social isolation and improved quality of life
- Increased patient confidence and independence
- Stronger partnerships with oxygen suppliers and travel industry
- Revenue stream from concierge service funds ongoing maintenance

### Supplier Search (Geocoding)

```javascript
// Node.js + Google Maps API
const axios = require('axios');

async function searchSuppliers(destination, radiusMiles, services) {
  // Geocode destination
  const geocode = await axios.get(
    `https://maps.googleapis.com/maps/api/geocode/json`,
    {
      params: {
        address: destination,
        key: process.env.GOOGLE_MAPS_API_KEY
      }
    }
  );

  const { lat, lng } = geocode.data.results[0].geometry.location;

  // Query suppliers within radius (using PostGIS or simple distance calc)
  const suppliers = await db.query(
    `
    SELECT *,
      (3959 * acos(cos(radians($1)) * cos(radians(latitude)) *
       cos(radians(longitude) - radians($2)) + sin(radians($1)) *
       sin(radians(latitude)))) AS distance
    FROM oxygen_suppliers
    WHERE services && $3
    HAVING distance < $4
    ORDER BY distance, rating DESC
  `,
    [lat, lng, services, radiusMiles]
  );

  return suppliers.rows;
}
```
