This document defines the **Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)** for Alpha1Life.com and specifications for the analytics dashboard that will track progress toward our strategic goals. **Primary Goals**:

4. Track fundraising and financial sustainability

### 2. Engagement Metrics (How people use the platform)

#### 2.1 User Engagement

**Monthly Active Users (MAU)** [North Star Metric]

- **Target Year 1**: 2,500 MAU
- **Target Year 3**: 8,000 MAU
- **Target Year 5**: 15,000 MAU

**Weekly Active Users (WAU)**

- **Target**: 60% of MAU (1,500 in Year 1)

**Daily Active Users (DAU)**

- **Target**: 20% of MAU (500 in Year 1)

**DAU/MAU Ratio** (Stickiness)

- **Definition**: Measures how "sticky" the platform is
- **Target**: 20%+ (excellent for community platform)
- **Calculation**: (DAU / MAU) Ã— 100

**Session Duration**

- **Definition**: Average time spent per session
- **Target**: 5-10 minutes (indicates engaged reading/posting)
- **Benchmark**: <2 min = bouncing, 5-10 min = engaged, >15 min = highly engaged

**Pages Per Session**

- **Target**: 4+ pages
- **Indicates**: Users exploring multiple resources, not just landing and leaving

#### 2.2 Forum Engagement

**Forum Registered Users**

- **Target Year 1**: 1,000 forum members
- **Target Year 3**: 3,500 forum members
- **Target Year 5**: 7,500 forum members

**Forum Activity Ratio**

- **Definition**: % of forum members who post/reply in a given month
- **Target**: 30%+ (high for forums; typical is 10-15%)
- **Calculation**: (Active posters / Total forum members) Ã— 100

**Posts & Replies**

- **Target Year 1**: 500 posts + 2,000 replies per month
- **Target Year 3**: 2,000 posts + 8,000 replies per month
- **Average**: 4 replies per post (indicates engagement, not dead threads)

**Response Time**

- **Definition**: Average time for first reply to a new post
- **Target**: <2 hours (especially for newly diagnosed questions)
- **Critical for**: Newly diagnosed patients feeling supported

**Top Forum Categories** (by activity)

- Newly Diagnosed
- Treatment & Clinical
- Living with AATD
- Family & Genetics
- Off-Topic / Community

**Moderator Activity**

- **Definition**: % of posts requiring moderation (spam, rule violations)
- **Target**: <5% (indicates healthy community)

#### 2.3 Content Engagement

**Patient Stories**

- **Definition**: Views per story, avg time on page
- **Target**: 500+ views per story in first month
- **Video Completion Rate**: 60%+ (for 2-5 minute videos)

**Resource Downloads**

- **Definition**: PDFs, guides, infographics downloaded
- **Target Year 1**: 2,000 downloads/month
- **Top Resources**: Treatment guide, genetic testing guide, financial assistance directory

**Newsletter Performance**

- **Open Rate**: 30%+ (industry avg: 21%)
- **Click-Through Rate (CTR)**: 5%+ (industry avg: 2.6%)
- **Unsubscribe Rate**: <1%

**Blog/Article Engagement**

- **Average Time on Page**: 3+ minutes (indicates reading, not skimming)
- **Social Shares**: 10+ shares per article
- **Comments**: 5+ comments per article

### 3. Retention Metrics (Do people come back?)

#### 3.1 User Retention

**Day 1 Retention**

- **Definition**: % of new users who return the day after signup
- **Target**: 40%+

**Day 7 Retention**

- **Definition**: % of new users who return within 7 days
- **Target**: 50%+

**Day 30 Retention**

- **Definition**: % of new users who return within 30 days
- **Target**: 60%+

**90-Day Retention**

- **Target**: 50%+ (excellent for health community)

**Cohort Retention Analysis**

- Track retention by cohort (month of signup)
- Identify which cohorts retain best (indicates successful features/content)

#### 3.2 Churn Rate

**Monthly Churn Rate**

- **Definition**: % of active users who don't return the following month
- **Target**: <20%
- **Calculation**: (Users who left / Total active users previous month) Ã— 100

**Reactivation Rate**

- **Definition**: % of churned users who return within 3 months
- **Target**: 15%+

---

### 4. Community Health Metrics

#### 4.1 Community Sentiment

**Net Promoter Score (NPS)**

- **Definition**: Survey question: "How likely are you to recommend Alpha1Life to another AATD patient?" (0-10 scale)
- **Target**: 50+ (excellent)
- **Calculation**: % Promoters (9-10) - % Detractors (0-6)
- **Survey Frequency**: Quarterly

**User Satisfaction (CSAT)**

- **Definition**: "How satisfied are you with Alpha1Life?" (1-5 scale)
- **Target**: 4.2+ / 5.0
- **Survey Frequency**: After key interactions (forum post, support group, story submission)

**Community Helpfulness**

- **Definition**: % of forum posts marked as "helpful" by original poster
- **Target**: 70%+

#### 4.2 Safety & Moderation

**Flagged Content**

- **Definition**: Posts/comments flagged by users or auto-moderated
- **Target**: <2% of total posts
- **Response Time**: <1 hour for review

**Banned Users**

- **Definition**: Users banned for violating guidelines
- **Target**: <0.5% of total users (indicates healthy community)

**Spam Rate**

- **Definition**: Spam posts/comments as % of total
- **Target**: <1%

---

### 5. Impact Metrics (Are we making a difference?)

#### 5.1 Patient Outcomes (Self-Reported)

**Time to First Support**

- **Definition**: Days from diagnosis to finding support on Alpha1Life
- **Target**: <14 days (ideally <7 days)

**Patient Knowledge Score**

- **Definition**: Survey question: "How confident do you feel in understanding your AATD diagnosis and treatment?" (1-10 scale)
- **Target**: 8+ (after 3 months of using Alpha1Life)
- **Baseline**: 4-5 (newly diagnosed)

**Reduced Isolation**

- **Definition**: Survey question: "I feel less alone with my AATD diagnosis thanks to Alpha1Life" (1-5 agree/disagree scale)
- **Target**: 4.5+ / 5.0

**Healthcare Engagement**

- **Definition**: "I feel more empowered to discuss my treatment with my doctor" (1-5 scale)
- **Target**: 4.2+ / 5.0

#### 5.2 Advocacy & Awareness

**Patient Stories Submitted**

- **Target Year 1**: 50 stories
- **Target Year 3**: 200 stories
- **Target Year 5**: 500 stories

**Social Media Reach**

- **Facebook Followers**: 2,000 (Year 1), 6,000 (Year 3), 12,000 (Year 5)
- **Instagram Followers**: 1,500 (Year 1), 5,000 (Year 3), 10,000 (Year 5)
- **Twitter Followers**: 800 (Year 1), 2,500 (Year 3), 5,000 (Year 5)

**Media Mentions**

- **Target Year 1**: 5 media mentions (blogs, news, podcasts)
- **Target Year 3**: 20 media mentions

### 6. Financial Sustainability Metrics

#### 6.1 Fundraising Performance

**Total Monthly Donations**

- **Target Month 3**: $2,000
- **Target Year 1 (Month 12)**: $8,750/month
- **Target Year 3**: $34,750/month
- **Target Year 5**: $58,333/month

**Donor Conversion Rate**

- **Definition**: % of active users who become donors
- **Target**: 20%+ (high for nonprofit, but community is highly motivated)

**Average Gift Size**

- **Target**: $75 (one-time), $35/month (recurring)

**Donor Retention Rate**

- **Definition**: % of donors who give again within 12 months
- **Target**: 60%+ (industry avg: 45%)

**Monthly Giving (Recurring Donors)**

- **Target Year 1**: 50 monthly donors ($1,750/month)
- **Target Year 3**: 200 monthly donors ($7,000/month)
- **Target Year 5**: 400 monthly donors ($14,000/month)

**Lifetime Value (LTV) per Donor**

- **Target**: $500+ over 5 years
- **Calculation**: (Avg donation Ã— Avg donations per year Ã— Avg donor lifespan)

#### 6.2 Fundraising Efficiency

**Cost to Acquire Donor (CAC)**

- **Target**: <$50 per donor
- **Calculation**: (Marketing spend + fundraising expenses) / New donors acquired

**Return on Investment (ROI)**

- **Target**: 5:1 (for every $1 spent on fundraising, raise $5)
- **Calculation**: Total donations / Fundraising expenses

**Donor Acquisition Channels**

- **Forum/Community**: 50% (organic, users become donors)
- **Email Campaigns**: 25%
- **Events**: 15%
- **Social Media**: 10%

**Platform**: Google Data Studio (free, integrates with GA, WordPress, Mailchimp)

## ðŸ“Š Reporting Cadence

### Daily Reports (Automated Email)

**Recipients**: Executive Director, Community Manager
**Metrics**:

- Visitors yesterday
- New signups
- Forum posts + replies
- Flagged content (if any)

### Weekly Reports (Dashboard Review)

**Recipients**: Executive team, Board liaison
**Metrics**:

- MAU (trend)
- Traffic sources
- Top content
- Donations this week

### Monthly Reports (Full Dashboard + Narrative)

**Recipients**: Full Board of Directors
**Format**: PDF report + dashboard link
**Includes**:

- Executive summary (narrative)
- All KPIs vs. targets
- Key wins and challenges
- Action items for next month

### Quarterly Reports (Strategic Review)

**Recipients**: Board, major donors, Patient Advisory Council
**Format**: Presentation + dashboard
**Includes**:

- Progress toward annual goals
- NPS and CSAT survey results
- Patient outcome stories (qualitative)
- Financial sustainability assessment
- Strategic recommendations
