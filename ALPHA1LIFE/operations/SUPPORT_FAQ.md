## 1Ô∏è‚É£ About Alpha1Life & Mark Egly Foundation

### Q: What is Alpha1Life.com?

**A**: Alpha1Life.com is a free online community and resource center for people affected by Alpha-1 Antitrypsin Deficiency (AATD). We provide:

- **Community Forums**: Connect with other patients, families, and caregivers
- **Patient Stories**: Real experiences from people living with AATD
- **Educational Resources**: Plain-language guides about AATD, treatment, and lifestyle
- **Support Groups**: Monthly virtual meetings
- **Specialist Directory**: Find AATD-experienced healthcare providers

Alpha1Life is run by the Mark Egly Foundation, a nonprofit 501(c)(3) organization dedicated to AATD awareness, education, and support.

---

### Q: Who is Mark Egly?

**A**: Mark Egly was a passionate advocate for Alpha-1 awareness who lived with AATD. He dedicated his life to educating others about the condition and supporting the AATD community. After his passing, the Mark Egly Foundation was established to continue his mission. Alpha1Life.com is one of our key initiatives to provide support and resources to the AATD community.

---

### Q: Is Alpha1Life.com affiliated with the Alpha-1 Foundation or AlphaNet?

**A**: No, we are an independent nonprofit organization. However, we collaborate with the Alpha-1 Foundation, AlphaNet, and other AATD organizations to better serve the community. We see ourselves as complementary‚Äîoffering a patient-centered community platform while they focus on research, advocacy, and clinical support.

We encourage you to connect with ALL resources available:

- **Alpha-1 Foundation**: Research, advocacy, testing programs
- **AlphaNet**: Case management, financial assistance
- **Alpha1Life**: Community, peer support, patient stories

---

### Q: Is Alpha1Life.com free to use?

**A**: Yes! Alpha1Life.com is 100% free for patients, families, and caregivers. You never need to pay to:

- Create an account
- Post in forums
- Access resources
- Attend virtual support groups
- Download guides

We are funded through donations from individuals, corporate sponsors, and grants. If you find value in Alpha1Life, we welcome (but never require) donations to help us continue our mission.

---

### Q: How is Alpha1Life.com funded?

**A**: We are funded through:

1. **Individual Donations**: From community members like you
2. **Corporate Sponsorships**: From pharmaceutical companies and healthcare organizations
3. **Grants**: From foundations supporting rare disease communities
4. **Fundraising Events**: Walks, galas, awareness campaigns

All funds support free services for the AATD community (website hosting, content creation, staff, support groups).

## 2Ô∏è‚É£ Account & Privacy

### Q: Do I have to use my real name?

**A**: No! You can use a username (like "SarahP" or "AATDWarrior") instead of your real name. Your privacy is important to us. Many members prefer to keep their identity private, and that's completely okay.

**What's visible to others**:

- Your username (you choose this)
- Your posts and replies
- Your profile picture (optional)

**What's private** (only visible to you and staff):

- Your email address
- Your real name (if you provided it)
- Your private messages

---

### Q: Is my health information private and secure?

**A**: Yes. We take your privacy very seriously and comply with HIPAA guidelines for handling health information.

**Our Privacy Protections**:

- **Encrypted Connection**: All data is transmitted via secure HTTPS
- **HIPAA-Compliant Hosting**: Our servers meet healthcare security standards
- **No Sharing**: We never sell or share your information with third parties
- **Staff Training**: All staff are trained in privacy and HIPAA compliance

**Important**: We recommend NOT posting detailed personal health information (PHI) in public forums. Use general terms:

- ‚úÖ Good: "I was diagnosed with AATD last year"
- ‚ùå Avoid: "My doctor is Dr. Smith at Stanford, and my FEV1 is 45%"

For privacy questions, see our [Privacy Policy].

---

### Q: Can I delete my account?

**A**: Yes. Email support@alpha1life.com and request account deletion. We'll permanently delete your account and personal information within 30 days.

**Note**: Your forum posts will remain visible (under your username) unless you request they be deleted as well. This preserves helpful information for the community, but we're happy to remove posts upon request.

## 3Ô∏è‚É£ Forum & Community

### Q: How do I post in the forum?

**A**:

1. **Create an Account**: Click "Sign Up" (takes 2 minutes)
2. **Choose a Forum Category**: Newly Diagnosed, Treatment, Living with AATD, etc.
3. **Click "New Topic"**
4. **Write Your Post**: Ask a question, share your story, seek advice
5. **Click "Submit"**

Your post will appear immediately (no approval needed). The community typically responds within a few hours.

---

### Q: What are the forum rules?

**A**: Our Community Guidelines ensure Alpha1Life is a safe, supportive space. Key rules:

‚úÖ **DO**:

- Be kind and supportive
- Respect others' experiences (everyone's AATD journey is different)
- Use "I" statements ("In my experience..." vs. "You should...")
- Flag inappropriate content (helps moderators keep community safe)

‚ùå **DON'T**:

- Give medical advice (share your experience, but clarify you're not a doctor)
- Post personal health info about others (HIPAA violation)
- Spam, advertise, or promote products
- Bully, harass, or attack others
- Share copyrighted content without permission

**Violations**: First offense = warning. Repeated violations = temporary or permanent ban.

Full guidelines: [Link to Community Guidelines]

---

### Q: Can I post anonymously?

**A**: You post under your username (which can be anonymous like "JohnD123"), not your real name. So yes, it's semi-anonymous.

However, everything you post is public and searchable. If you share specific details about your life, someone might be able to identify you.

**For truly private conversations**: Use Direct Messages (DMs) to specific members or email staff.

---

### Q: Someone posted something offensive. What do I do?

**A**:

1. **Flag the post**: Click the "Flag" button on the post
2. **Explain why**: "This violates community guidelines because..."
3. **Moderators review**: Within 1-2 hours, a moderator will review and take action

**Don't**: Engage with trolls or argue publicly. Let moderators handle it.

---

### Q: How do I find others with similar experiences?

**A**:

- **Forum Search**: Use search bar to find posts about specific topics (e.g., "augmentation therapy")
- **Forum Categories**: Browse categories like "Newly Diagnosed" or "ZZ Genotype"
- **Introduce Yourself**: Post in "Introductions" forum‚Äîothers will respond
- **Virtual Support Groups**: Monthly Zoom meetings (connect in real-time)

---

### Q: What if I post something I regret?

**A**: You can edit or delete your own posts within 24 hours of posting. After that, contact a moderator or email support@alpha1life.com to request deletion.

## 4Ô∏è‚É£ Medical/Clinical Questions

### Q: Can I ask medical questions in the forum?

**A**: You can ask questions about others' experiences, but remember:

- **We are NOT doctors**: No one here can provide medical advice
- **Peer support only**: We share personal experiences, not clinical guidance
- **Always consult your doctor**: For medical decisions, talk to your healthcare provider

**Good forum questions**:

- "Has anyone experienced side effects from augmentation therapy?"
- "What questions should I ask my doctor about starting treatment?"
- "How do you manage shortness of breath day-to-day?"

**Questions for your doctor** (not the forum):

- "Should I start augmentation therapy?"
- "Is this symptom related to my AATD?"
- "Can I stop taking this medication?"

## 6Ô∏è‚É£ Technical Support

### Q: The website is slow or not loading. What's wrong?

**A**: Try these steps:

1. **Refresh the page**: Press Ctrl+R (Windows) or Cmd+R (Mac)
2. **Clear your browser cache**: Settings ‚Üí Privacy ‚Üí Clear browsing data
3. **Try a different browser**: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge
4. **Check your internet connection**: Is WiFi working?
5. **Check our status**: [alpha1life.com/status] (if site is down for everyone)

Still having issues? Email support@alpha1life.com with:

- What you were trying to do
- Error message (screenshot if possible)
- Browser and device you're using

---

### Q: I'm not receiving email notifications from the forum. Why?

**A**:

1. **Check spam/junk folder**: Our emails sometimes get filtered
2. **Add us to contacts**: Add notifications@alpha1life.com to your contacts
3. **Check your notification settings**: Profile ‚Üí Settings ‚Üí Email Notifications (make sure they're turned on)
4. **Verify email address**: Profile ‚Üí Settings ‚Üí Email (confirm it's correct)

Still not working? Email support@alpha1life.com.

---

### Q: How do I upload a profile picture?

**A**:

1. Click your username (top right) ‚Üí Profile
2. Click "Edit Profile"
3. Under "Profile Picture," click "Upload"
4. Choose image (JPG or PNG, max 2MB)
5. Click "Save Changes"

---

### Q: The forum isn't working on my phone. Help!

**A**: Alpha1Life.com is mobile-friendly. If you're having issues:

- **Update your browser**: Use latest version of Chrome, Safari, or Firefox
- **Clear mobile cache**: Settings ‚Üí Browser ‚Üí Clear cache
- **Try desktop view**: If mobile view has issues, switch to desktop view in browser settings
- **Report bug**: Email support@alpha1life.com with phone model and browser

---

### Q: Can I access Alpha1Life on my smartphone?

**A**: Yes! Alpha1Life.com works on smartphones and tablets. Just visit alpha1life.com in your mobile browser (Chrome, Safari, etc.).

We don't currently have a mobile app, but the website is optimized for mobile use.

---

### Q: How do I report a technical bug?

**A**: Email support@alpha1life.com with:

- **What happened**: Describe the issue
- **Steps to reproduce**: What did you do before the error occurred?
- **Screenshot**: If possible, include a screenshot of the error
- **Browser/device**: What browser and device are you using?

We typically respond within 24 hours and aim to fix bugs within 1 week.
