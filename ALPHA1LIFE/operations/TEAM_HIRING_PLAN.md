This document outlines the **staffing roadmap** for Alpha1Life.com from beta launch through Year 5, including organizational structure, job descriptions, compensation ranges, and hiring timeline. The plan balances immediate launch needs with long-term sustainability and growth.

**Staffing Philosophy**:

- Start lean (3 FTEs at launch), scale based on metrics
- Hire for mission alignment and cultural fit, train for skills
- Competitive compensation (50th percentile nonprofit sector)
- Blend of staff + volunteers + contractors for flexibility

**Day in the Life**:

- 9:00 AM: Review forum overnight activity, respond to flagged posts
- 10:00 AM: Reply to new member introductions, welcome posts
- 11:00 AM: Moderate forum (spam, guideline violations)
- 12:00 PM: Call with patient for story interview
- 1:00 PM: Edit and publish patient story video
- 2:00 PM: Facilitate virtual support group (Zoom)
- 3:00 PM: Train new volunteer moderators
- 4:00 PM: Review community health dashboard, report to ED

## ðŸ’° Compensation & Benefits

### Salary Philosophy

- **Target**: 50th percentile for nonprofit sector (based on Guidestar/Charity Navigator benchmarks)
- **Geography**: Adjust for location (remote workers in high COL areas)
- **Experience**: Salary ranges account for 3-5 year experience spread

### Benefits Package (Full-Time Employees)

**Health Insurance**:

- Employer pays 80% of employee premium
- Options: HSA-eligible high-deductible plan OR PPO
- Estimated cost: $600-$800/month per employee

**Retirement**:

- 403(b) plan with 3% employer match (after 1 year)
- Vesting: Immediate

**Paid Time Off**:

- Vacation: 15 days Year 1, 20 days Year 2+
- Sick Leave: 10 days per year
- Holidays: 12 federal holidays + 2 floating holidays
- Personal Days: 2 per year

**Professional Development**:

- $2,000/year per employee for conferences, training, certifications

**Remote Work**:

- Fully remote positions available (location-flexible)
- Home office stipend: $500 one-time setup

**Other Benefits**:

- Flexible schedules (core hours 10am-3pm, flexible start/end)
- Mental health support (EAP - Employee Assistance Program)
- Student loan repayment assistance (if funding allows, Year 3+)

## ðŸ¤ Volunteer Program

### Volunteer Roles

**Forum Moderators** (5-10 volunteers)

- **Responsibilities**: Monitor forum daily, respond to flags, enforce guidelines
- **Time Commitment**: 5-10 hours/week
- **Requirements**: Active forum member 3+ months, trusted by community
- **Training**: 2-hour training on guidelines, moderation tools
- **Recognition**: "Forum Moderator" badge, annual appreciation event

**Patient Ambassadors** (10-20 volunteers)

- **Responsibilities**: Share patient stories, participate in campaigns, provide feedback
- **Time Commitment**: 2-5 hours/month
- **Requirements**: Willing to share story publicly
- **Recognition**: Featured on website, t-shirt, annual event invitation

**Event Volunteers** (50+ volunteers)

- **Responsibilities**: Help with walks, galas, awareness events
- **Time Commitment**: 1-2 events per year (4-8 hours each)
- **Recognition**: T-shirt, certificate, social media shoutout

### Volunteer Management

**Recruitment**: Open applications on website, promote in newsletter/forum
**Onboarding**: Welcome email, training session, assign buddy (existing volunteer)
**Recognition**: Monthly spotlight, annual volunteer appreciation event, gifts
**Retention**: Check-ins, feedback surveys, clear communication
