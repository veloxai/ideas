## ðŸŽ¯ Executive Summary

This Customer Success Playbook provides **standardized protocols, templates, and best practices** for supporting AATD patients and families throughout their journey with Alpha1Life.com. The goal is to ensure every patient feels heard, supported, and empowered to manage their condition.

**Core Principles**:

1. **Patient-Centered**: Every decision prioritizes patient needs and experience
2. **Responsive**: Quick response times (<2 hours for forum posts, <24 hours for emails)
3. **Empathetic**: Recognize emotional needs (fear, isolation, overwhelm)
4. **Empowering**: Provide tools and knowledge, not just answers
5. **Community-Driven**: Connect patients with each other, not just staff

## ðŸ“ž Communication Channels & Response Times

### Forum Posts

- **Response Time Target**: <2 hours during business hours (9am-5pm ET), <24 hours nights/weekends
- **Who Responds**: Community Manager, Moderators, Community Members
- **Escalation**: Crisis posts (suicidal ideation, medical emergency) escalate to ED immediately

### Email (support@alpha1life.com)

- **Response Time Target**: <24 hours weekdays, <48 hours weekends
- **Who Responds**: Community Manager
- **Common Topics**: Account issues, technical problems, partnership inquiries, donation questions

### Private Messages (Forum DMs)

- **Response Time Target**: <48 hours
- **Who Responds**: Community Manager, Moderators (for moderation issues)
- **Note**: Encourage users to post publicly when possible (benefits whole community)

### Phone

- **Availability**: By appointment only (not a crisis hotline)
- **Use Cases**: Major donors, partnership discussions, media inquiries, sensitive patient concerns
- **Referral**: For crisis support, refer to Alpha-1 Foundation helpline or National Suicide Prevention Lifeline

### Social Media (Facebook, Instagram, Twitter)

- **Response Time Target**: <24 hours
- **Who Responds**: Community Manager or Marketing Manager
- **Common Topics**: Questions about platform, sharing patient stories, fundraising campaigns

## ðŸ“‹ Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs)

### SOP 1: Onboarding New Volunteer Moderators

**Step 1: Recruitment**

- Post opening in forum
- Require: Active member 3+ months, trusted by community, 5+ hours/week commitment

**Step 2: Application Review**

- Review forum history (helpful, kind, follows guidelines)
- Phone interview with Community Manager (30 min)

**Step 3: Training**

- 2-hour training session (guidelines, moderation tools, crisis protocols)
- Shadow existing moderator for 1 week
- Start with limited permissions, full access after 2 weeks

**Step 4: Ongoing Support**

- Monthly moderator meetings (share challenges, best practices)
- Access to private moderator forum
- Direct line to Community Manager for questions

---

### SOP 2: Publishing Patient Stories

**Step 1: Recruitment**

- Post call for stories in forum, newsletter, social media
- Email past contributors to ask for referrals

**Step 2: Initial Outreach**

- Email or call potential storyteller
- Explain process, time commitment, privacy options
- Get verbal yes before proceeding

**Step 3: Interview**

- Schedule 30-60 min phone or Zoom call
- Ask open-ended questions (diagnosis story, challenges, hopes, advice for others)
- Record (with permission) or take detailed notes

**Step 4: Writing/Editing**

- Draft story (500-1,000 words) in patient's voice
- Send to patient for review and approval
- Make edits as requested
- Get written consent for publication

**Step 5: Publication**

- Add to website (Patient Stories section)
- Share in newsletter, social media, forum
- Send thank-you gift (Alpha1Life t-shirt or mug)
- Follow up after 1 month (how has response been?)

---

### SOP 3: Monthly Virtual Support Group

**2 Weeks Before**:

- Email all members (forum + newsletter list)
- Post in forum with RSVP link
- Social media announcement

**1 Week Before**:

- Reminder email to RSVPs
- Forum reminder post

**Day Before**:

- Confirmation email with Zoom link + agenda

**Day Of**:

- Test Zoom setup 30 min before
- Start 5 min early to welcome early arrivals
- Facilitate discussion (use agenda, encourage participation, keep on time)
- Record (with permission) for those who can't attend

**After**:

- Thank-you email to attendees
- Post recording (unlisted YouTube link) in forum
- Solicit feedback via survey
- Document attendance, topics discussed
