## ğŸ¯ Project Overview

**Alpha1Life.com** is a comprehensive patient-facing website that will serve as the digital home for people affected by Alpha-1 Antitrypsin Deficiency (AATD). The platform combines patient education, community support, and fundraising to fulfill the Mark Egly Foundation's mission of supporting Alpha-1 patients and families.

---

## ğŸ’¡ The Opportunity

**Current State**: old.alpha1life.com is a minimal single-page site with just a tagline and donation button.

**Vision**: Transform into a comprehensive patient resource that rivals MichaelJFox.org and Alpha1.org, becoming the premier patient-facing AATD community hub.

**Gap We Fill**:

- Existing sites (Alpha1.org) are clinical and research-focused
- Newly diagnosed patients need simple, compassionate guidance
- Families need community connection and peer support
- Patients want to share stories and help others

## ğŸ¨ What We're Building

### Core Features

**1. Patient Education Hub** (70 pages)

- "What is AATD?" in plain language (6th-8th grade reading level)
- "I Just Got Diagnosed" step-by-step guide
- Treatment options explained simply
- Living well resources
- Family genetic testing info

**2. Community Forums** (HIPAA-compliant)

- Safe space for patients to connect
- Anonymous posting option
- 6 forum categories (newly diagnosed, living with AATD, caregivers, treatment, clinical trials, off-topic)
- Professional moderation
- Private messaging between members

**3. Patient Stories**

- Video testimonials
- Written stories
- Photo galleries
- "Share Your Story" submission form
- Celebrate hope and resilience

**4. Donation Platform**

- One-time and recurring gifts
- Impact-driven giving ($25 = 1 patient helped)
- "Alpha-1 Champions" monthly giving circle
- Peer-to-peer fundraising tools
- Honor/memorial gifts

**5. Specialist Directory**

- Searchable database of AATD-experienced doctors
- Filter by specialty and location
- Patient reviews (moderated)
- Integration with Alpha-1 Foundation directory

**6. Support Group Locator**

- Find in-person groups nearby
- Virtual support groups
- "Start a Group" toolkit

**7. Resources**

- Downloadable guides
- Videos and webinars
- FAQ
- Glossary
- Testing locations

## âš ï¸ Risks & Mitigation

### Risk 1: Low Initial Traffic

**Mitigation**:

- Partner with Alpha-1 Foundation for cross-promotion
- SEO optimization from day one
- Social media campaigns
- Email outreach to existing MEF contacts

### Risk 2: Forum Moderation Challenges

**Mitigation**:

- Recruit 3-5 volunteer moderators before launch
- Clear community guidelines
- HIPAA training for all moderators
- Auto-moderation tools (spam filters)

### Risk 3: Donor Fatigue/Competition

**Mitigation**:

- Differentiate from Alpha-1 Foundation (local focus, patient stories)
- Strong stewardship and donor communication
- Diverse revenue streams (individual, corporate, grants)

### Risk 4: Technical Issues/Downtime

**Mitigation**:

- Premium hosting with 99.9% uptime guarantee
- Daily backups
- 24/7 monitoring
- Developer on retainer for urgent issues

### Risk 5: Budget Overruns

**Mitigation**:

- Detailed project plan with milestones
- Phase approach (can pause between phases if needed)
- Fixed-price contracts with developers
- Monthly budget review
